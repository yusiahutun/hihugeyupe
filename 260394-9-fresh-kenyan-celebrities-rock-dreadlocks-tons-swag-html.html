9 fresh-looking Kenyan celebrities who rock dreadlocks with tons of swag

Publish date: 2024-05-24

Over the years, dreadlocks have evolved from just being a socio-political statement to being a fashionable hairstyle with several twists to it, literally.

Celebrities seem to have warmed up to the trend that was in the past associated to our maumau forefathers and a few daring individuals who dared to rock it.

In the recent past, you'd be considered as queer if you happened to be spotted trying to pull off the now popular hairstyle.
