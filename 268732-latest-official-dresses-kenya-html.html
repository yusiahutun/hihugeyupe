Latest official dresses in Kenya

Publish date: 2024-05-28

Someone once said, how you dress determines how people will address you. For this reason, strive to look the part no matter the occasion. At work, you should aim for clothes that give you a professional look but are still trendy, fashionable, and a perfect fit for your body. So where can you find the best official dresses in Kenya?

Finding formal dresses in Kenya can be an easy but challenging task. First, there are the expensive designer dresses available in select fashion stores in the country. Then, there are the other mass-produced clothes available at the nearest boutiques.

For lovers of unique but slightly affordable garments, there is always the option of “camera” from Gikomba, Toi, or your local market. Alternatively, you can shop online from local and international stores. No matter where you buy, always make sure you find a dress that makes you look good and feel great.

Official dresses for ladies in Kenya

Some employers may provide a standard dress code while others are flexible. In general, formal dresses should be long enough (about two inches below the knees), and not too tight. Choose dresses made from natural fibers such as cotton and wool as they are of higher quality compared to the synthetic ones. Also, check for the layer underneath; you do not want clothing that shows your undergarments. Unless you work in a sector that limits your color choice, feel free to experiment with color. Lastly, always find a dress that suits your body type.

There are a variety of formal dresses that can be worn by women of different sizes and shapes. Here some of the latest official dresses in Kenya.

1. Peplum dress

The peplum dress has been in style for a while now. Peplum dress gives a two-piece (skirt and top) effect when worn. When worn, the peplum adds volume to your hips as it creates extra horizontal lines across the area and shortens your leg length. The ideal height for a formal peplum dress is mid-length. You can accessorize the dress with a belt and a nice pair of heels or ankle boots. Peplum dresses come in a variety of color and shapes. Some of the colors to wear to work other than black or grey are maroon, royal blue, and rosewood. You can purchase peplum dresses from online stores such as Jumia, Kilimall, and Mimi from KES 1500.

2. Pencil dress

Just like the pencil skirt, the pencil dress enhances your curve. However, the design for these dresses favors mostly the curvy but slender figures or hourglass bodies, but can also be worn by curvy and plump ladies. The pencil dress complements your features and still gives you the same elegant as the business suit. Remember, the dress should not be too tight as it can expose your undergarments. Also, buy one that is double-layered and made from sturdy fabric such as cotton. The pencil dresses come in a variety of designs. You can get the dresses from online stores such as Kilimall, Jumia, and eBay from KES 1400, Gikomba from KES 2500.

3. Skater dress

With a full bottom and a fitting top, the skater dress complements slim and size body. The dress is ideal for pear-shaped bodies. The magic with the skater dress is that it makes make full figured women smaller and adds volume for skinny women. The options available in the market are plain-colored, stripped, floral, multicolored, and pleated among others. You can wear the dress with a slim belt or a statement neckpiece or earrings. Scatter dresses in Kenya are mostly available from online shops such as Kilimall, Jumia, eBay, from KES 1800.

4. Suit dress

Who says you need two pieces when you can achieve the same look with one? As the name suggests, a suit dress is a replica of a suit. The dresses have a variety of designs that work with most body sizes. You can purchase the suit-dress from online stores such as Kilimall, Jumia, and eBay from KES 1800.

5. Sheath dress

The primary features of the sheath dress are high necklines and fitted waists. The dresses vary in length, but the most appropriate is mid-length hemline. Plus, the sheath dresses can have short or long sleeves. Above all, the sheath dress is perfect for most body types. You can wear the clothing with a matching blazer and a payer of heels.

Alternatively, you can style the sheath dress with a beautiful silk scarf or statement neck piece. In Kenya, you can purchase sheath dresses from online stores or local markets from KES 1500, Vivo from KES 3950, and Kache by Angie KES 7500.

Official maternity dresses in Kenya

Dressing up the baby bump can be fun with the right dresses. With formal pregnancy dresses, shape and the trimester are vital factors. During the first trimester, you can wear your loose-fitting usual clothes from your wardrobe. For the other trimesters, here are some formal dresses for pregnant ladies.

i. Tank dress

The tank dress flatters your bump and your curves. You can accessorize it with a neckpiece or a matching blazer. You can get tank dresses from Alibaba, eBay, for about $20. Also, on OLX the maternity circle neck tank dress goes for Ksh. 799.

ii. Shift dress

This dress works well with any body size and shapes. Since it hangs from the shoulders, you can wear a shift dress in any trimester. You can find shift dresses from online shops like eBay, Alibaba, and Amazon from $10.
