Mesut Ozil biography

Mesut Ozil was born on October 15th, 1988 in Gelsenkirchen, German. He is a grandson of a Turkish immigrant and the son of Mustafa Ozil, and Gulizer Ozil. He has three siblings; a brother who goes by the name Mutlu Ozil and two sisters, Dugyu Ozil and Nese Ozil.

Early life

Mesut developed his soccer skills alongside his friends at what they called a ‘Monkey Cage,’ a local pitch surrounded by a fence. He played for several small youth programs before he got lucky to join the Academy of Gelsenkirchen’s Football Club Schalke 04.

Career and stats

In the year 2005, Ozil made it to Schalke 04 youth academy. He represented the team and played as an attacking midfielder. Despite his promising talent, in 2008 he was allowed to transfer to SC Werder Bremen. The player flourished in his new club assisting the club to get in the finals against Bayern Munchen in 2009 DFB cup. Bayern Munchen, however, proved too good for them hammering them 4 goals to nil.

Personal life

Mesut is a third generation Turkish-German. Because of his Turkish descent, he attended Gesamtschule Berger Feld. He was eligible to play for both German and Turkish, however, at age 17, he decided on the German citizenship and started playing for the German U-19 team.

What Religion is Mesut Ozil?

Mesut practices Muslim and this could be seen through his usual recitation of the Quran before his matches. He also observes the Islamic fasting of Ramadan.

Mesut Ozil girlfriend

In 2013, he began dating Mandy Caprino. The relationship however ended in October 2014 following the report of Ozil being involved with another woman. The two made up but later broke up in 2017.

Ozil began dating former Miss Turkey, Amine Gulse. She later became his wife on June 7th, 2019.
