Who is Frank Fritz?

Frank was born on 11th October 1965 in Davenport, Iowa. He attended a local high school but never went to college. The TV personality is also a tattoo fanatic. He has multiple tattoos on his arms, with some being done by James Hawk of ''Hawk's Tattoos''. The show host had inked his left arm but now has full sleeve designs on both his arms after paying a visit to Hawk.

The American picker started his career by working as a safety and fire inspector for about 25 years.
