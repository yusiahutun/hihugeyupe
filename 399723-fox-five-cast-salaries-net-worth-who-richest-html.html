Fox The Five cast salaries and net worth: Who is the richest?

Publish date: 2024-06-25

The Five is a hugely popular American panel talk show that airs on the Fox News Channel. Five panellists discuss current pop culture, political issues, and trending stories on the show. The one-hour show premiered in 2011 and airs every weekday from 5 pm. Like other top talk shows with several panellists, The Five has brilliant co-hosts who keep their viewers glued to the screen. What are the current Fox The Five cast salaries, and who is the richest among them?

The show has four permanent co-hosts and seven regulars who rotate on the liberal and conservative seats. At any given time, there are five panellists (four permanent and one regular on the liberal or conservative seat).

Fox The Five cast salaries

Here is a look at the current co-hosts of The Five, their annual salaries, and current net worth.

Greg Gutfeld is a renowned editor, producer, blogger, broadcaster, and journalist. He joined The Five in 2011 and is one of the show's longest-serving hosts. According to Celebrity Net Worth, Greg Gutfeld's net worth in 2021 is $13 million. He reportedly earns $7 million in annual salary. Besides co-hosting The Five, Gutfeld is also a former host of The Greg Gutfeld Show.

Today, he hosts Gutfeld! on the Fox News channel. On the show, he parodies current events and analyses trending issues with a touch of humour. He also regularly interviews pop culture icons and newsmakers on The One podcast.

Gutfeld began his career as a media assistant in an intern position. Since then, he has worked for numerous internet and print media companies.

Dana is an accomplished news anchor, journalist, and author. She also served in George W Bush's government as the White House Press Secretary. The news anchor joined The Five in 2011. At the time, some of the show's viewers were unaware of her previous high-profile career in the government.

Why did Juan Williams leave The Five?

The 67-year-old left The Five after serving as the show's liberal voice for close to 7 years. He said his decision to leave was informed by the plans to resume in-person studio appearances. These had been put on hold due to COVID-19.

Juan Williams' net worth in 2022 is $2 million.

Who is Greg from The Five married to?

The Fox News television host is married to Elena Moussa. The two met when Greg was working in London. After dating for a few months, they tied the knot in 2004 in a civil wedding.
