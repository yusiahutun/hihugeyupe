Kane Brown's biography

The singer was born on 21st October 1993 in Northwest Georgia. His mother is Tabatha Brown, but his father's name is not known to the public. He has one sibling known as Heidi Swafford.

Is Kane Brown black? He is not black but multiracial. His mother is white while his father is African-American. Brown's family moved to Northwest Georgia while he was growing up and even stayed homeless at one time.

Kane attended Lakeview-Fort Oglethorpe High School and Soddy Daisy High School. After graduation, he started living with his grandmother, who played a huge role in raising him.

Career

Kane auditioned for American Idol and The X Factor. He was selected for The X Factor but later quit after the producers wanted to include him in a boy band. He started posting cover versions of popular songs on social media.
