What happened to Frankie J? Everything you need to know

Publish date: 2024-03-17

Francisco Javier Bautista Jr, otherwise known as Frankie J, is a Mexican-American musician, songwriter, and a former member of the musical group Kumbia Kings. Frankie’s exceptional talent in hitting high vocal cords in Spanish and English is remarkable. He is one of the Latino pioneers to sing R&B and has re-emerged recently with a fresh twist to his music. So, what happened to Frankie J?

Frankie J released his solo debut album What’s A Man To Do in 2003. In 2013, the pop star received a Grammy Award for the Best Latin Pop Album.

In his former years as an artist, Frankie has always been associated with the R&B genre. However, his recently launched album is different from what fans are accustomed to hearing.

R&B singer Frankie J latest album

Canciones Que Recuerdo is Frankie’s latest album. The album means a lot to him, and it was launched seven years after winning the Best Pop Latin Album in the 2013 Grammy Awards.

Frankie J and Baby Bash history

As noted earlier, Frankie has been active in the music industry for almost two decades. He traces his roots from his family members that consisted of all musicians. Growing up in a family of musicians, Frankie got inspired to pursue music, and he is a remarkable vocalist.
