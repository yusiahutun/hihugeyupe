15 Disney stars who went to rehab due to drug addiction

Publish date: 2024-07-29

Growing up as a Disney child star seems like a dream come true. Many former stars have attained financial independence at a young age, not to mention the acting career prospects that crop up. But, unfortunately, things don't work out so well for some, and they find themselves facing the Disney curse. Are you familiar with some of the Disney stars who went to rehab?

The "Disney curse" is a name for a phenomenon that seems to affect several former Disney channel stars. Due to the pressure and extravagant life they are exposed to when young, some of these stars face problems later, drug abuse and addiction being one of them.

15 Disney stars who went to rehab

The pressure of the spotlight has often proven too much for some of the Disney stars. As a result, some of them end up engaging in drug and substance abuse. Below is a list of famous Disney stars who ended up in rehab due to drugs or substance abuse. Some of them have managed to turn their lives around, while others are still fighting addiction issues.

1. Zac Efron

Zac Efron is one of the most famous Disney stars of recent times. He grew up on the Disney Channel set, appearing in films and shows such as The Replacements, High School Musical, and The Suite Life of Zack & Cody. However, he had a hard time coping with his fame at such a young age, causing him to turn to alcohol and drugs.

In 2013, Zac Efron Disney's golden boy reputation suffered a massive hit after entering rehab for alcohol and cocaine addiction. Luckily, the young actor got clean and is now sober. He has also re-invented his career and has starred in top films such as Baywatch, The Beach Bum ,and Dirty Grandpa.

2. Shia LaBeouf

Shia LaBeouf is another one of the top male Disney stars that have gone to rehab. He became famous for, among others, his roles on the Disney series Even Stevens. He also featured in the 2003 Disney film Holes. Unfortunately, the actor has struggled with substance abuse stemming from a young age.

3. Selena Gomez

Selena Gomez is a singer and actress who became famous for her roles on Disney Channel. She first appeared in the show the Suite Life of Zack & Cody before being cast in Hanna Montana. However, it was her role as Alex Russo on Wizards of Waverly Place that made her famous.

Unfortunately, the former Disney Princess and American's sweetheart has not been spared by the Disney curse. In 2014, she checked into a rehab facility, leading to rumours of a possible drug problem. However, she has since opened up, revealing that she was seeking treatment for mental health issues. More recently, in 2018, she checked into rehab for addiction, but not due to drugs – social media.

4. Demi Lovato

Demi Lovato is an American singer and actress and one of the most famous Disney child stars.
