What does de Angelis mean?

De Angelis is an Italian name from the personal name Angelo. The name means a member of the Angeli family. It can also be interpreted to imply from the angels.

The name also has a rich history in Italy. The city of Venice had many noble families, including the De Angelis family. Individuals coming from this area were recognized by one name, but it became necessary later for people to get a second name as the population grew. And that is how the process of adopting a fixed hereditary name came up in Italy in the 10th and 11th centuries.

De Angelis is thought to have been a nickname meaning an angelic person or someone who acted as a religious messenger.
