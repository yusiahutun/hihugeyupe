Publish date: 2024-01-31

For many years, Audi has maintained its tradition of using celebrities to advertise some of its vehicles. Besides, celebrity marketing is not new since it helps sell products. The logic is that people idolise celebrities, and once they promote a new product on the market, they get motivated to buy it. So, if you have watched Audi ads, chances are you have seen a celebrity driving the car or narrating the best feature of the automobile. So, who is in the Audi commercial?

Actors and actresses in Audi commercials

Who are the actors and actresses in Audi ads? Audi has used many actors and actresses in its ads to promote its cars. If you are keen enough to follow the ads, you may have noticed the following celebrities.

1. Regé-Jean Page

Regé-Jean Page is an English actor born on 27 April 1988 (age 34 as of 2022) in London, England. He is best known for portraying Simon Basset in Bridgerton, a Netflix period drama series. Furthermore, he has played Leonard Knox in For The People (2018-2019), a legal drama TV series.

Regé-Jean Page appears as the actor in the Audi Q4 commercial titled Celebrating Progress, marketing the Audi Q4 e-Tron. The ad came out in 2021 to introduce Audi’s new line of electric vehicles.

2. Janelle Monáe

Janelle Monáe is an American singer, songwriter, rapper, author, and actress born on 1 December 1985 (age 36 as of 2022) in Kansas City, Kansas, United States. Although she is an accomplished singer with eight Grammy Award nominations, she is notable for her work as Jacqueline Calico in the second season of Homecoming (2020).

Janelle Monáe is the black woman in the Audi commercial presenting the Audi RS e-Tron GT released in 2021.

3. Maisie Williams

Maisie Williams was born Margaret Constance Williams on 15 April 1997 (age 25 as of 2022) in Bristol, England. She is an English actress well known for portraying Arya Stark in Game of Thrones (2011-2019).

Maisie has played Rahne Sinclair/Wolfsbane in The Mutants (2020) and Kim Noakes in Two Weeks to Live (2020). She is the woman in the Audi commercial singing Let It Go. She appeared in the ad promoting the Audi e-Tron Sportback during the 2020 Super Bowl.

4. Tom Hardy

Tom Hardy was born Edward Thomas Hardy on 15 September 1977 (age 44 as of 2022) in Hammersmith, London, England. He is an English actor, producer, screenwriter, and former model known for his work as Alfie Solomons in Peaky Blinders (2014-2022) and Al Capone in Capone (2020). He appears in an Audi advert promoting the Audi e-Tron GT for the Ireland market.

5. Claire Danes

Claire Danes was born Claire Catherine Danes on 12 April 1979 (age 43 as of 2022) in New York City, United States. She is an American actress known for her role as Carrie Mathison in the Showtime drama series Homeland (2011-2020).

She is a Primetime Emmy, Golden Globe, and Screen Actors Guild Awards winner. Claire appeared in an Audi ad in 2013 for the Audi TDI on a night she won an Emmy.

6. Andy Lau Tak-wah

Andy Lau Tak-wah is a Hong Kong actor, singer-songwriter, and film producer born on 27 September 1961 (age 60 as of 2022). He is a commercially successful film actor who has performed in more than 160 films. Furthermore, Andy maintains a successful music career even in 2022.

In 2022, Andy appeared in a controversial Audi ad for the Hong Kong market, accused of copyright infringement. Audi removed the ad and apologised for the video campaign.

7. Theo James

Theo James is an English actor born Theodore Peter James Kinnaird Taptiklis on 16 December 1984 (age 37 as of 2022) in High Wycombe, Buckinghamshire, England. He is well known for his role as Tobias "Four" Eaton in The Divergent Series film trilogy.

James has starred in many films and TV shows, including Underworld: Awakening (2012), How It Ends (2018), and The Time Traveler's Wife (2022). In 2014, Theo James was the voice in a United Kingdom ad for the Audi A6 Ultra. However, he has offered his voice in more Audi ads, including Audi A3 Sportback and Audi A4.

8. Tom Holland

Tom Holland is an English actor born Thomas Stanley Holland on 1 June 1996 (age 26 as of 2022) in London, England. He is best known for playing Spider-Man in six Marvel Cinematic Universe (MCU) superhero films, beginning with Captain America: Civil War (2016).

He has also appeared in other films, including Edge of Winter (2016) and The Devil All the Time (2020). He also received the British Academy Film Award and Guinness World Record. In 2017, he appeared in an Audi ad titled Peter's Driving Test.

9. Kristofer Hivju

Kristofer Hivju is a Norwegian actor born on 7 December 1978 (age 43 as of 2022) in Oslo, Norway. He is best known for his roles as Tormund Giantsbane in Games of Thrones (2013-2019) and Connor Rhodes in The Fate of the Furious (2017). Hivju has also appeared in other projects, including The Witcher (2021) and Downhill (2020).

Kristofer is the actor in the Audi commercial for Norway advertising the Audi e-Tron, the best-selling car in Norway. He made three ads for the same vehicle: Salmon Fight, Norway Loves Pizza, and Norway Says "No Way" To Will Ferrell.

10. Michael Maino

Michael Maino is an American actor born in Jersey City, New Jersey, United States. He is of Italian descent and has appeared in many films, including Brooklyn 62nd (2020), Which is Witch (2015) and The Cigar Collector (2011).

Michael Maino is the man in the Audi ad titled Test Drive, released in 2017. His excellent description of the car and the general production of the video allowed the ad to receive a nomination for Online Commercial of the Year at the Shots Awards in London.

11. Desi Lydic

Desi Lydic was born Lani Desmonet on 30 June 1981 (age 40 as of 2022) in Louisville, Kentucky, United States. She is an American comedian and actress best known as the correspondent on The Daily Show with Trevor Noah. Lydic has also appeared in other projects, including Legally Blonde 2: Red, White & Blonde (2003) and Awkward (2011-2016) as Valerie Marks.

12. Toby Huss

Tobias Huss is an American actor born on 9 December 1966 (age 55 as of 2022) in Marshalltown, Iowa, United States. He is best known for playing Clyde Sukeforth in the biographical drama film 42 (2013), John Bosworth in Halt and Catch Fire (2014-2017), and Edward Dickinson in Dickinson (2019-2021). In addition, Toby Huss appears in the Audi A7 commercial titled Nightwatchman.

For example, you may have noticed many celebrities in Audi commercial promoting a new vehicle or creating awareness of new features introduced in their automobiles. The most notable ones are Toby Huss, Desi Lydic, Michael Maino, Kristofer Hivju, Peter Parker, Theo James, and Janelle Monáe.
