GHRIS online payslip download, registration and printing guide

Publish date: 2024-05-16

People employed by the government have, in the past, had to deal with tedious processes on issues of human resources. Such experiences became alarming following the ever-growing labour force that somehow had gone out of hand. It is then that the government developed GHRIS to provide an easy platform for employees to access online services.

Following the concern that government employees needed online services with ease, the Government of Kenya developed an online portal where their staff could efficiently access them. This system is known as Government Human Resource Information System, commonly referred to as GHRIS. This platform has brought convenience and efficiency, ensuring government employees get their payslips at the comfort of their homes or workplaces.

What you need to know about GHRIS Kenya?

GHRIS stands for Government Human Resource Information System. The main aim of GHRIS is to address the need for a human resource system for the government employees. It is a kind of portal where the employees of government can download their payslips.

This portal is designed in-house by the officers of Government Information Communication Technology in association with officers of Human Resource. The main users of Government Human Resource Information System are mainly employees, Government MDAs and other people. They will be getting benefits from a centralized, easily accessible and efficient system.

What was the motivation towards GHRIS development?

The government embarked on the development of GHRIS to address all its Human Resource requirements. It was designed internally by the Government Information Communication Technology Officers in partnership with human resource officers. The portal interfaces with other current and future systems such as IPPD, G-PAY, IFMIS, and IRMIS.

Who are the users of GHRIS?

The beneficiaries of GHRIS payslip download include all civil servants in both county and national governments. These include county employees and teachers. Others are medical professionals, administration officers, among other state employees. The boon about this system is that it is safe from invasion or vulnerabilities and therefore works through an effective privacy policy with clear terms of use.

How to register and download payslips from GHRIS

Below are the steps on how to register on GHRIS and download a payslip from it.

Registration

There are few easy steps which you have to follow by users for the registration. The following steps for registration have been given below:

  • The first step for registration is to visit the official website of Government Human Resource Information System by following this link.
  • The login page will be shown on your screen. You will get to see three options that are given, such as; forgot password, register and log in here. You have to click on the register button if you are a new user and if you are continuing member, click on login button.
  • After that, a registration form will appear on to your screen. Fill up the employee registration form carefully by providing your personal details like; mobile number, national number, KRA pin, date of birth, work details such as the term of services and appointment date, gender and official name. After providing all the details you need to click the submit button.
  • The information of yours will be received by respective government agency, department or ministry. By the respective employer grievances and disparities are addressed. If no such issue arises then GHRIS payslip download Kenya is always at your service.

    GHRIS online payslip download

    Before you download your GHRIS payslip on the portal, you are required to have two essential items. These are GHRIS user personal number and GHRIS password. Below is a step by step guide on how to download and print your GHRIS payslip:

  • Access the GHRIS official website.
  • Click on the Government of Kenya Employees link.
  • Fill in your ID number and password. Then click log in.
  • If the details you have provided are correct, the portal opens.
  • Click on My Records tab.
  • Choose my payslip option.
  • Several existing payslips will appear on the left side of the screen.
  • If you only need your current slip, select the presentpayslip link.
  • A new page will open up with the option of the month, year, and view payslip. Select view payslip and wait for it to be displayed.
  • Select the option, "print payslip" to print your payslip. Choose your desired printer and finally click okay.
