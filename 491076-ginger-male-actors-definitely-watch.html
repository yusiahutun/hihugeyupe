15 ginger male actors you should definitely watch in 2024

Publish date: 2024-04-14

A ginger male is a man whose hair is red. Red hair is not common in the human population, and only about 1-2 percent of humans were born with it. Most people with red hair are in Europe. And in the entertainment scene, the number of ginger male actors is significantly low. However, when these individuals appear in a TV show or movie, they always stand out because of their unique looks.

While it is widely known that ginger male actors have red hair, the red hair varies in hue. In other words, some ginger men have bright copper hair, and others burn orange hair colour.

You will also meet some actors with strawberry blond, red-orange, or burgundy hair. However, the consensus is that the actors have a high reddish pigment on their hair. And although some may have dark hair, it is not easily noticeable.

15 ginger male actors

The movie industry has some of the most famous celebrities with ginger hair. Which male actors have ginger hair? Conan O’Brien, Simon Pegg, David Wenham, Zack Ward, Fran Kranz, and Paul Bettany are examples of male actors with ginger hair. Here are 15 other actors with red hair to watch out for in 2024.

1. Alan Tudyk

Alan Tudyk is an American actor known for portraying Gerhardt in 28 Days (2000) and Wash in Serenity (2005). In 2021, Alan took on the role of Dr Harry Vanderspeigle in Resident Alien and continued the role for season three in 2023.

People often confuse Alan Tudyk’s hair with being blonde or strawberry blonde. But Alan has always admitted to being ginger.

2. Benedict Cumberbatch

Benedict Cumberbatch is one of the many British ginger-haired male actors. The award-winning actor is well known for his role as Sherlock Holmes in the BBC series Sherlock (2010-2017).
