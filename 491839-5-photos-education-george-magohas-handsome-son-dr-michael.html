5 Photos of Former Education CS George Magoha's Handsome Son Michael

Publish date: 2024-03-29

George Magoha has left behind a wife, Barbra, and a son, Michael.

Michael Magoha is happily married and always wears his wedding ring.

A look at Michael's Instagram account shows an outgoing young man, who loves life.

Earlier TUKO.co.ke reported that the friend to the family of the late CS narrated the events before his death.

In the initial instances of Magoha's worrying trend, his son Michael, a medical doctor, would try resuscitating him, but all would ultimately be in vain.

Other colleague doctors' efforts to reinforce to bolster Michael failed too.

"He greeted me and said "ooh you have come", I started taking his blood pressure, but it was not recording, after some time he slept off from his sitting position and we took him to the car. The wife and my driver followed. They were ahead of us about 200 metres," Mwanda said.

Adding that;

"From the information, he collapsed, and he was resuscitated by his son who is a medical doctor then 100 metres to the casualty of Nairobi Hospital he collapsed again, and he was rushed to casualty, there was resuscitation which took some time but were unable to resuscitate him."
