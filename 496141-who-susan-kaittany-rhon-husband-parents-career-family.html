Who is Susan Kaittany from RHON? Husband, parents, career, family

Real Housewives of Nairobi (RHON) is the latest reality TV show in Kenya and is already trending. The show's cast comprises famous reality TV stars such as Vera Sidika and features new faces. One of these new faces is Susan Kaittany.

Susan Kaittany is a Kenyan entrepreneur famous for being the CEO of the beauty parlour Posh Palace. She is also a former model as well as a lawyer by profession. Despite being famous for her beautician business, Kaittany is still a mystery, especially concerning her personal life.

Early life

The model was born around 1984, making her approximately 39 years old as of 2023. However, she is yet to reveal her date of birth. She grew up in the Langata area in an average Kenyan family. Her father was a public servant, while her mother (Rosemary Kaittany) was a teacher, earning Ksh8,000 monthly.

Not much is known about her father, but her mother later quit her job to start a travel agency. Today, she is the owner and managing director of the agency – ATS Travel. Kaittany grew up alongside her siblings, though she has yet to reveal much about them.

She was raised in a strict family, where her mother – a teacher – maintained discipline. In an interview with Standard Media, she praised her for teaching her (and her siblings) to work hard, which led to her success. Susan attended Langata West primary school, where her mother used to teach.

Later, she attended Makini High School in Nairobi for her secondary education, where she was into activities such as fashion, arts, and sports – she was the basketball team captain. After completing her secondary education, she enrolled at Moi University in Eldoret, where she pursued a degree course in law.

Career

Growing up, Susan Kaittany always had a passion for fashion. This drove her to the world of modelling, and especially beauty pageants. In 2004, she competed in the Miss World Kenya pageant, where she was one of the finalists. She also participated in the 2005 Miss Earth pageant, where she emerged as the winner and was chosen to represent Kenya internationally.

Kaittany grew disillusioned with the modelling world due to poor payment and the mistreatment of models in the industry. She then turned to commercials, a job that she found more fulfilling. She did this while she was still in college studying law. Besides commercials, Kaittany also started working in her family's business – a travel agency they had launched.

She still works in the company, where she currently serves as one of the board members. Another business Susan Kaittany is famous for is Posh Palace, a hair and beauty salon based in Kilimani, Kenya. She started the business with her former friend Betty Kyallo before the two had a falling out.

After Kyallo left the business, Kaittany became its full owner and is currently its CEO. In 2021, she expanded the business by launching a luxury perfume line, Polished by Posh. Kaittany ventured into another line of work – a reality TV show. She is one of the main cast members of the TV show Real Housewives of Nairobi, which started airing on the Showmax network on February 2023.
