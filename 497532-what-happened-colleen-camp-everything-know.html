Colleen Camp’s biography

Colleen Camp was born Colleen Celeste Camp on 7th June 1953 in San Francisco, California, USA. The American character actress is 69 years old as of 2023. She is the daughter of Dorothy Camp (mother) and Wilson Camp (father). He has two siblings, Glen and Don Camp (brothers).

Her family relocated from San Francisco to San Fernando Valley when she was still a young girl. After completing her primary school education, she joined John H Francis Polytechnic High School. Later she went to Los Angeles Valley College before joining California State University, Northridge. She majored in English and minored in theatre arts.

Career

Colleen started working while still in college to help pay for her fee. She worked as a macaw's trainer at the Busch Gardens on weekends, weeknights, and during summer. She performed for up to 2,000 people in six shows every day, six days a week.
