20 best barrel dreads styles for men to try out in 2024

Publish date: 2024-08-27

Are you looking for unique dreadlocks hairstyles for men? The barrel dreads are the go-to option because they are stylish and low maintenance. When you search for styles to try out, you will notice so many styling possibilities. But which are the best barrel dreads styles for men in 2024?

Gone are the days when the dreadlocks hairstyle was reserved for African American men. Nowadays, the hairstyle has been embraced by men from different races in the world. There are so many dreadlocks hairstyles you can try, including barrel dreads and twists.

List of barrel dreads styles

From barrel roll short dreads to barrel braids, the list of barrel dread styles you can try is endless. If you are stuck on styling options, there is no need to panic. These dread styles for men will serve as an inspiration.

1. Half up, half down barrel twists

This is one of the timeless dreads styles that you need to try this year. The hairstyle is easy to achieve. You only need to part your barrel dreads into two, the front part and the back part. Then, tie up the barrel dreads in the front part into a bun. The remaining barrel twists at the back should be left hanging.

2. Barrel roll short dreads cornrows

This is one of the easiest barrel dreads styles you can pull off if you prefer short and simple hairstyles. To achieve this look, plait several cornrows out of the barrel dreads. The classic and timeless hairstyle will make you stand out from the crowd.

3. 2 barrel twist dreads

If you want to make a statement with your barrel dreads, the 2 barrel twists hairstyle should be at the top of your list. To achieve this look, ask your stylist to create two large twists out of your long locs.

4. Individual barrel twist dreads

If you have long locs, you can create several individual twists out of them. The style is easy to achieve. You only need to part your locs into several sections. Then, twist the locs in each section to create one big loc, as shown in the picture above.

5. 3 barrel braids

If you don't want your barrel locs to hang freely, you can create three cute cornrows out of them. The style is simple to recreate. You only need to divide the locs into three sections running from the front part of your head to the back. Then, plait one big cornrow in each section.

6. 2 barrel braids

The list of styling options for barrel dreads also includes 2 barrel braids. To achieve this look, you will follow the same procedure for the 3 barrel braids but divide the locs into two sections. The style is ideal if you are looking for something simple.

7. Barrel dreads with a touch of blonde

If you want something fancy and convenient, this style is the go-to option. The style is easy to achieve. You only need to create cute lines stretching to the middle part of your head. Then, dye the barrel dreads with a blonde colour.

8. Barrel twist mohawk

If you are looking for a cool way to rock your barrel dreads, the mohawk is the go-to option. You can recreate the style shown in the picture or experiment with different mohawk styles. The clean, shaved sides will make a strong statement.

9. 4 barrel twist dreads cornrows

There is no restriction on the number of cornrows you can create from the barrel locs. You can create 4 barrel dreads cornrows like the guy shown in the picture. The hairstyle is the go-to option if you don't want something wild and shouting.

10. Barrel dreads low bun

If this is the first time you are wearing barrel dreads and you don't have the courage to try wild hairstyles, this is the go-to option. Gather all the locs together in the lower part of your head to create a low bun.

11. Cute barrel with dreads hairstyle

If you are looking for an attention-grabbing style for your barrel dreads, you can recreate the style in the picture. The best aspect of this hairstyle is that holds the locs in place. You do not need to worry about the locs falling around your face. This cute hairstyle will make you look groomed up and elegant.

12. Long braided barrel twist dreadlocks

Long barrel twists are one of the hottest hairstyles for men. If you want the hairstyle to look catchy, you should create multiple cornrows out of them, just like in the picture above. This cute hairstyle will definitely turn heads.

13. Colourful barrel dreads styles

If you are tired of the traditional black barrel dreads, this is a perfect styling idea for you. The trick here is to have your locs dyed with vibrant colours. Adding a pop of colour to your locs is the go-to option if you are looking for an eye-catching and vibrant look.

14. Barrel dreads ponytail

If you are looking for a unique way to style your long barrel dreads, you should consider the ponytail. You can pull them into a high ponytail like in the picture. The look is ideal for you if you are looking for a style that will keep locs off your face and shoulders.

15. Medium barrel dreads with shaved sides

If you want to spice up your barrel dreads look, you can add an undercut to it. The hairstyle is easy to recreate. Ask your stylist to shave the sides of your head and create cornrows out of the remaining hair.

16. Barrel dreads multiple buns

The barrel dreads style is quite versatile. You can create multiple buns out of the locs. To recreate this look, divide your locs into several sections. Then, create cute buns in each section.

17. Half barrel rolls with undercut

This is one of the trendy and unique hairstyles you need to try this year. For this look, shave the sides and back of your head. Then, create locs out of the remaining hair and style them as shown in the picture above.

18. Barrel dreads with blue ends

If you are looking for an attention-grabbing style, the barrel dreads with blue ends are the go-to option. The trick here is to dye the ends of the barrel dreads dyed using a blue dye. The style will make you the centre of attention.

19. Extra long barrel dreads

If you love long hair, you can go for the extra-long barrel dreads. The important thing to note about this hairstyle is that it is time-consuming. Therefore, you should be prepared to spend more hours in the braider's chair. But, the end result will be a style that will turn heads.

20. Short barrel dreads

If none of the styling options appeals to you, you can go for short locs. Even though the short locs are not as appealing, they are low maintenance and easy to style. You can leave the locs to hang freely, as shown in the picture. The freely hanging locs style looks effortless.
