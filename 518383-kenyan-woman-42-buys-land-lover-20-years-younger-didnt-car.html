Land buying in Kenya

Land buying in Kenya is a contentious issue that has left many people feeling the pain of losing millions in land deals gone bad.

Kenyan lawyers and investment experts have urged Kenyans to do due diligence, including land searches at the Ministry of Land, to avoid buying public land disguised as private property.

Individuals duped in land-buying deals have often ended up losing their property and even having their houses demolished when courts issue orders in favour of genuine owners.
