Who was Elizabeth Montgomery?

Elizabeth was a renowned American actress. She was born on 15 April 1933 in Los Angeles, California. Her parents were Broadway actress Elizabeth Allen and film star Robert Montgomery. Her elder sister died in infancy, but she had a younger brother, Robert B Montgomery Jr.

She attended Westlake School for Girls in Holmby Hills, California, before graduating from the Spence School in New York City. Later, she joined the American Academy of Dramatic Arts in Manhattan, where she studied for three years.

Elizabeth was famous for appearing in highly acclaimed movies, TV shows, stage performances, and narrations. She debuted acting in her father's TV show, Robert Montgomery Presents, from 1951 to 1956. In October 1953, she made her Broadway debut starring Janet Colby in Late Love.
