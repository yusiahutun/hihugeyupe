Linet Toto Builds Parents Multi-Million Bungalow, Shares Beautiful Photos

Publish date: 2024-04-02
  • Linet Toto Chepkorir, Bomet woman representative, was over the moon with the progress of her parents' house
  • The young politician shared pictures of the multi-million bungalow she is building for her adorable parents
  • Social media users congratulated her for putting her family first and gifting them a precious gift

Most kids believe one of their biggest successes is to gift or build their parents a house to show their gratitude.

Bomet woman representative Linet Toto Chepkorir has shown off the lovely gift she has prepared for her parents.

The politician has built her amazing parents a beautiful bungalow worth millions of Kenyan shillings.

"My gift to my parents is at an advanced stage.I thank God for this far. When God bless you, remember your parents," her caption read.

Toto appreciates God for house progress

The beautiful young lady shared lovely pictures of the magnificent bungalow, which looked amazing.

The environment looked peaceful and green as trees encompassed the lovely house built with quality materials.

It had vibrant colours, and the compound looked vast, but it was yet to be adequately fenced.

Toto noted in her viral post that it was good progress, appreciating God for the journey.

Nairobi evangelist list ways to appreciate your parents

Emmanuel Muuo, a resident of Nairobi shared similar thoughts with Toto on building a house for his parents.

However, the young evangelist at Kingdom Network advised that building a house for one's parents was not the only way to show appreciation to them.

"With the high cost of living and different priorities in life, not many can afford to build houses for their parents. You can even shop for the house, gift your parents some clothes or vouchers. There are many ways to show you value your parents, publicly or secretly. I would also love to build my parents a good home but only time will tell," he told TUKO.co.ke.
