Christina Shusho Discloses She Left Her Matrimonial Home to Focus on Ministry: &quot;Ni Assignment&quot;

Publish date: 2024-02-28
  • Gospel singer Christina Shusho has opened up about splitting with her husband and the reason that led to their breakup
  • She explained that she asked her husband to let her go so that she could focus on what God had called her to do
  • Social media users expressed mixed reactions after her revelation, with some urging her to make amends with her husband

Tanzanian singer Christina Shusho has opened up about parting ways with her husband so she could focus on her calling.

Christina Shusho on parting ways with hubby

The Shusha Nyavu hitmaker was in an interview with New Jerusalem Church TV, where she said she had a dream that she hoped to see come alive and revealed they had an amicable split.

"I have never been fake, and I don't lie. The truth is, it's just an assignment. There is nothing different other than that. The assignment God has given me this season cannot allow me to stay where I was. I had to leave to go fulfil the assignment," she said.

She also maintained that she is not a pastor and expressed discomfort at being referred to as a preacher.

Is Shusho on good terms with Hubby

She also shared that she wants to achieve everything she set out to do and revealed that she promotes the pastors in her life.

"I am someone with a dream. There is something I must see. I don't like people calling me a pastor. I think I have more than I must do," she added.

Shusho also narrated that she asked her hubby to release her so that she could go and fulfil her calling, and she revealed that they were still on good terms.

She also disputed that God can give a man and woman related calling and said everybody has their paths to follow even as they support each other.
