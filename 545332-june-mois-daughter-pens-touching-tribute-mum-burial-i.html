June Moi's Daughter Pens Touching Tribute to Mum on Her Burial: &quot;I Miss Her&quot;

Publish date: 2024-05-28
  • June Chebet Moi will be laid to rest on Thursday, April 18, at her native home in Bahati, Nakuru county
  • The deceased's body was airlifted from Nairobi to the Kabarak Airstrip, accompanied by the bereaved family
  • The daughter of June, Paula Jepkemboi, gave a touching tribute to her late mum, stating that she still loved her and will forever miss her
  • Her funeral was attended by prominent personalities such as Kalonzo Musyoka, Eugene Wamalwa and David Maraga

Paula Jepkemboi, the daughter of the late June Chebet Moi, has broken down while remembering her memories with her mum.

Her remains were airlifted from Nairobi to her native home in Kabarak on the morning of Thursday, April 18.

Ranking political leaders, including Wiper leader Kalonzo Musyoka and DAP-K's Eugene Wamalwa, accompanied the body beside the family.

Did June Moi have a daughter?

June was a mother of one and first laid her eyes on her gorgeous daughter, Paula Jepkemboi in 2004.

Jepkemboi showered her mum with love in her tribute.

She said:

"If Roses Grow in Heaven. If roses grow in heaven, Lord, please pick a bunch for me. Place them in my mother's arms and tell her they're from me. Tell her I love her and miss her, and when she turns to smile, place a kiss upon her cheek and hold her for a while. -Because remembering her is easy. I do it every day, - but there's an ache within my heart that will never go away. Mama, I promise to be strong and make you proud.You did so much and more and all for me. I am so happy you are with your own Mama, Kuka, Kogo, Brother and your best friend, Uncle Patchy. - I will miss you more than anything. And if one day I am able to be half the woman you were, I would consider - myself lucky. Daughter Paula Susanne Jepkemboi."

When did June Moi die?

June was announced dead by the late Daniel Moi's family on Thursday, April 11, aged 60 years.

She succumbed to a bout of an undisclosed illness; she was the last-born daughter of former president Daniel Arap Moi.

The family requested privacy from Kenyans as they mourned their loved one. The late retired president adopted her at a young age.

June attended the Nairobi Primary School and later proceeded to Kenya High School for her A levels before moving to Canada to pursue her tertiary education.

Moi had seven biological children: Jennifer Chemutai Kositany, Doris Elizabeth Chepkorir, Jonathan Moi (now deceased), Raymond Moi, Philip Moi, John Mark Moi, and Gideon Moi.

President William Ruto was among the leaders who commiserated with the family immediately after the sad news was broken.
