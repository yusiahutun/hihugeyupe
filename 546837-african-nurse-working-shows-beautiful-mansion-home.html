African Nurse Working In US Shows Off Beautiful Mansion Back at Home

Publish date: 2024-07-05
  • A young African man working in the US as a nurse has displayed his new mansion erected in his backyard back in Africa
  • The man was captured in a video shared online admiring his new crib while some renovation work was being done
  • Many netizens who came across his video congratulated him on his achievements, recognising his efforts in looking for greener pastures

A man's search for greener pastures abroad seems to have paid off, as he built a beautiful house in his homeland.

The young man, a nurse in the US, showed videos of the interior of his new crib in Accra, which is fully tiled and fitted with sofas, TV sets, ACs, dining sets, and decor fit for modern houses.

The young man was captured admiring the fruits of his labour while some workers were also seen replacing the inner doors with new ones.

US family impressed by house built by relative

In related news, a family living in the USA came back home to Africa and was so impressed by the house their relative built for them.

According to Odugwu Charles, who shared the post on Facebook, the older brother sent home money, and his younger brother faithfully used it to build a house for him.

Among the jaw-dropping features of the house are a big swimming pool, big bedrooms, and luxury bathrooms.
