Publish date: 2024-04-15

Little Miss Shirtless!

Former child star Abigail Breslin is the latest actress to take a couple risque/controversial pics with celebrity photographer Tyler Shields -- going topless in a new shoot.

Breslin, 17, can be seen preserving her modesty with a white sheet in one photo, with a "Lolita"-like red lollipop in her mouth.
