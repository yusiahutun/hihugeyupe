Amal Clooney Embodies Elegance in a Minidress and Perfect Mary Janes

Publish date: 2024-09-07

Amal Clooney's latest street style look is the epitome of sophistication.

The human rights lawyer was spotted out and about in New York City earlier this week, when she cut an elegant figure in an ivory white minidress with a mock turtleneck. She stayed warm by layering up in a pair of sheer black tights and a long black coat covered in white speckles. The outerwear additionally included bulky silver buttons and large boxy pockets.

To cap off the look, Amal wore the perfect pair of Mary Janes. The heeled black shoes featured pointed toes, double straps, block heels, and a glossy finish. She further accessorized with a black leather Dior tote bag and black cat-eye sunglasses.

Fans of Amal's street style can likely recognize her penchant for clean, minimalist, and elevated pieces.

For instance, during an outing in New York City last year, the lawyer opted for a monochromatic moment in a sleek ivory trench coat and a matching shift dress. She contrasted the ensemble's color palette with her accessories, including a black handbag, croc-embossed heels, studded earrings, and an oversized pair of tinted sunglasses.

During another appearance with husband George Clooney last year, Amal again opted for neutral tones, this time wearing a ribbed knit turtleneck sweater dress in a creamy hue. She styled the midi-length dress with black heeled knee-high boots with a red trim, oversized sunglasses, and a white cross-body bag with gold hardware.
