Ana Villafae Exclusive Portraits Hollywood Life

Publish date: 2024-06-12

Ana Villafañe is an American actress and singer from Miami, FL who made her Broadway debut as ‘Gloria Estefan’ in the Tony nominated hit bio-musical about Gloria’s life, On Your Feet! in 2015. As a newcomer who was discovered through a tape she submitted for an open casting call, Ana was met with rave reviews and nominated for the Outer Critics Circle Award, Drama League Award and Astaire Awards. Following the show’s closure in 2017, Ana went on to act join the cast of New Amsterdam in season 2 as Dr. Valentina Castro. She also will join Kal Penn in the recurring role of Diana Barea in his upcoming NBC comedy series, Sunnyside.
