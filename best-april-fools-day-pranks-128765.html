Best April Fools' Day Pranks For Your Grandkids

Publish date: 2024-08-26

You might think that silly jokes are just for the little ones, but when it comes to the best April Fools’ Day pranks, even grandparents have a few tricks up their sleeves!

As we all know, kids can be quite clever when it comes to fooling their relatives. But this time around, you can join in on the fun by preparing a few awesome jokes of your own, whether that means planting a few surprises in their morning breakfast or swapping out their favorite desserts for veggies. For one, you’ll get some of the best reactions (it wouldn’t hurt to keep your camera nearby!), and second, preparing these easy pranks will take practically no time at all!

So if you’re still feeling stuck or need some ideas to get you started, don’t worry! We’ve rounded up a list of hilarious jokes that even the most clever grandkids would fall for. Keep in mind that some of them just might try to retaliate (and who can blame them?) — so be prepared to celebrate with a possible prank war!
