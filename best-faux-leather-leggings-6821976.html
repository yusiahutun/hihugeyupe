The 16 Best Faux Leather Leggings of 2024

Publish date: 2024-08-24

Faux leather leggings just may be the MVP of our closets — and for good reason. “Faux leather pants are a timeless staple because the look will never change drastically from season to season and can be styled in many different ways,” celebrity stylist Ebony Brown tells InStyle. 

Unlike faux leather pants, which have come back in a big way recently, these chic, shiny leggings are form-fitting and usually high-waisted. But beyond those two mainstays, several unique details, cuts, and designs separate basic faux leather leggings from pairs that will stand out in a crowd. We asked fashion mavens to recommend their favorites of the faux trend, and we shimmied in and out of several pairs ourselves to see what truly passed muster. (Read: We made sure you can actually get them on and off your body easily, unlike what poor Ross experienced in that one Friends episode.)

Best Overall

Spanx Faux Leather Leggings

Spanx

What We Love: They’re 2-in-1 shapewear and stylish pants.

What We Don't Love: They run a bit small.

Spanx can do no wrong, especially with these pants made of nylon, elastane, and spandex blend. But don’t let the faux label fool you — these feel heavy like natural leather, meaning they don’t turn sheer or appear see-through. These shiny high-rise pants are compressing but also super smooth and breathable. They have typical shapewear properties — like making your backside look good — but not constricting, so you are still able to move in them. Because they are shapewear-esque, they run a bit small because they’re tighter than most. We recommend sizing up if you don’t want as much compression. Otherwise, they offer the right amount of control and functionality. Not to mention, they’re low-maintenance with washes. (Just throw ‘em in a machine on cold, and you’re ready for another wear.) They also offer short, regular, and tall sizes, checking all our boxes for the top spot on our list.

“The faux leather finish adds a trendy texture, while Spanx's shaping technology provides a flattering fit,” says Alicia Lloyd, styling supervisor at Stitch Fix. “The leggings offer versatility for both casual and dressed-up looks, making them a wardrobe staple that accentuates the figure and boosts confidence.”

Sizes: XS-3XL | Colors: 1 | Texture: Smooth | Length: Regular, Petite, Tall

We probably wouldn’t wear them to the gym, so we’re not too concerned about the squat test.

Size: 0-14 | Colors: 1 | Texture: Smooth | Length: Regular

What To Keep In Mind

Texture

Quality faux leather will have a deep, grainy texture but feel soft like genuine leather. But regardless of the texture — whether you prefer a patent (like Commando Faux Patent), a matte finish (like Good American Good Waist Faux Leather Leggings), or something in between like Spanx Faux Leather Leggings— it should feel sleek and ultra-smooth on the body because it will most likely be lined.

Stretch

If you’re looking for faux leather leggings that move with the body or are made for workouts, look for a four-way stretch label. Brands like Commando, Spanx, and Alo have high-stretch fabrics and a spandex-y feel that move with your body. They also offer high-waist bands great for movement. With its breathable, four-way stretch material, Carbon38 Legging in Takara Shine is our pick for Best Workout.

Rise

Most faux leather leggings that made our list have a high-waist or mid-rise, which we love as the higher rise helps to create the illusion of longer legs. The extra coverage also can help smooth your middle, especially in a shaping style like Yummie Faux Leather Shaping Legging with Side Zip.

Your Questions, Answered

How do you style faux leather leggings?

The reason to invest in a pair of faux leather leggings or pants? You can style them any way you feel like. “The styling possibilities are endless,” says celebrity stylist and costume designer Ebony Brown. Her favorite is a faux leather trouser styled with a cropped top and an oversized blazer, à la workwear casual. But if you’re looking for something edgier, Brown says go for a ‘90s vintage t-shirt and faux leather joggers. “This is a cute street style moment for my clients, usually paired with high-heeled boots or sneakers, depending on the mood,” says Brown. Faux leather leggings worn with a pair of stylish rain boots can even elevate your rainy-day fashion.

What shoes look best with faux leather leggings?

As always, wear what makes you comfortable, but if you’re looking for an editor-approved look or a stylist’s recommendation, the experts think that faux leather leggings look their best with boots. “Tall boots and platform boots can elevate the look,” says Brown. But for something more casual, a sleek pair of white sneakers will do the trick.

How do you wash faux leather leggings?

You’re lucky: Vegan and faux leather are much easier to clean than genuine leather. Removal of stains can be done with warm water and a microfiber cloth. As for a full scrub down, you may want to check if your pleather pieces are machine washed. Most pieces don’t come with the drab of dry-cleaning; Some can be thrown right into the washing machine on cold. For pairs that can’t, hand scrub in cool water with a gentle cleanser until no suds remain. Turn inside out and repeat. Ensure no excessive detergent is left, as this can cause it to stiffen and crack. Then finally, air dry.
