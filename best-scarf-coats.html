Although we’re not too fond of sweating during the summer months, the season does have its perks. Fewer layers, for one, and less extras to go along with them — hats, gloves, scarves, etc., which we usually end up shoving into the sleeves of our coat the second we slip it off. But this year, we’re ready for a change. While some days might call for bulky puffers, we’re also getting great use out of scarf coats, AKA winter’s ultimate one-and-done piece.

In theory, the design is pretty genius: rather than keeping track of multiple items, the scarf is simply attached to your outerwear. In action, it looks sleek, sophisticated, and feels kind of fancy, especially if the scarf is oversized and can be draped over your shoulder. Although this coat trend has been bubbling up for a bit, 2024 is the year you’re bound to spot it everywhere, especially with the TikTok popularity of Toteme’s version, which costs a cool $1,130.

The good news is that you don’t have to have a ton of money to buy one of your own. We’ve seen scarf coats at every price range, whether we’re talking somewhere in the hundreds to under-$100. Here are a few of our favorites.
