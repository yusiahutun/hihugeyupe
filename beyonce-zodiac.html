No one can deny that Beyoncé’s overall effect is carefully tailored, leaving nothing to chance. This is definitely not by accident, as Beyoncé is *obsessed* with making her work an intricate experience that’s open to interpretation. There’s always tons of symbolism in her music, encouraging hordes of fans to pick apart every metaphor, every lyric and every performance, knowing there must be a deeper meaning behind it all.

Beyoncé’s secretive, formidable and transformative Scorpio moon makes her someone who’s incredibly in tune with her intuition.
