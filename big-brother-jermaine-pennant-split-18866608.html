Jermaine Pennant 'SPLITS' with wife Alice Goodwin six months after Chloe Ayling scandal

Publish date: 2024-04-11

Jermaine Pennant and his wife Alice Goodwin have reportedly called it quits, six months after he flirted with Chloe Ayling on national TV.

According to the Mirror, the pair broke up over Christmas.

The footballer, 36, and the brunette bombshell have since removed all traces of each other from their respective social media accounts, as well as unfollowing each other.

Alice and Jermaine have also ditched their wedding rings in recent weeks.

Alice's wedding ring – which Jermaine added an extra £50,000 worth of diamonds too following his CBB stint – has been missing from her recent posts.

Jermaine's wedding ring has also not been visible in his latest photo updates of his golfing holiday to Turkey.

The news of an alleged split comes after Alice, 32, shared a cryptic Instagram post about life being "tricky".

She also shared a sentimental Valentine's Day message to her 325k Instagram followers, which read: "Happy Valentine’s Day.

"You deserve the kind of love that makes you feel like the luckiest person alive, that people envy and admire, that makes you love yourself."

The news of their reported breakup comes just six months after Jermaine got cosy with kidnapped model Chloe Ayling, 21, in the Celebrity Big Brother house.

Chloe and Jermaine flirted up a storm in the famous Borehamwood pad, with the glamour model unaware that Jermaine was married to Alice.

Shortly after Jermaine's CBB appearance, himself and Alice appeared on The Jeremy Kyle Show and vowed to salvage their marriage after his flirting antics.

Jermaine and Alice married in 2014. The former Arsenal footie star was engaged three times before he met Alice, and has a son from a previous relationship.

Jermaine's spokesman denied reports of a split when contacted by Daily Star Online.
