Blake Lively's daughters just met 'Wolverine' in surprise Deadpool 3 visit

Publish date: 2024-07-15

Blake Lively surprised her husband Ryan Reynolds and brought two of their daughters to the Deadpool 3 movie set while the actor films the next part in the film series in England.

Ryan Reynolds is a man of many roles, with his business ventures as a co-owner of the Wrexham club and the alcoholic brand Aviation Gin. The Hollywood star is currently filming Deadpool 3 and his wife and two of their children surprised him on the set of the third part of the franchise.

Blake Lively brings daughters to Deadpool 3 set

The Age of Adaline actress, 35, brought two of their daughters to the set of Deadpool 3 to surprise Ryan and meet his co-star Hugh Jackman, who plays Wolverine in the upcoming movie.

Blake Lively sported casual attire with a white shirt and leather jeans, while one of her daughters was seen with a couple of books under her arm as Ryan walked up to meet his family.

One of the viral snaps showed a sweet interaction between Ryan, one of his daughters, and Hugh, who was dressed in character in a stunning yellow suit and holding a sword. Here are the adorable snaps of Ryan and his daughters meeting Wolverine on set.

Ryan and Hugh are filming in England

The new pictures come amid Ryan and Hugh filming scenes of Deadpool 3 in England. Production started in May in London and at Pinewood Studios, while other scenes will be filmed in Canada.

Ryan returns as the titular character in the red suit and Hugh, who is a good friend of the celebrity Deadpool star off-screen, joined the standalone movie as Wolverine. Deadpool and Wolverine have officially joined the Marvel Cinematic Universe after Disney bought 20th Century Fox.
