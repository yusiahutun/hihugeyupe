Bobbi Althoff deletes viral Drake interview days after attending concert

Publish date: 2024-09-23

Just days after she attended his concert, Bobbi Althoff and Drake have now unfollowed each other on social media, with his viral interview for her The Really Good Podcast, being deleted from YouTube. Of course, fans are desperate to know what happened.

Ever since the interview was released, it seemed fans had found their new favorite duo. Since the interview, Althoff has gone on to interview music stars including Tyga and Offset, but unfortunately, it seems like those hoping to listen back to the Drake interview won’t be able to.

Bobbi Althoff and Drake’s podcast episode deleted from YouTube

Previously, Bobbi mentioned how she DM’d Drake on Instagram after seeing he’d liked her posts and followed her, and took her chances by asking him to come on the podcast. Two days later, she had flown over to Memphis to interview him ‘under the covers’ and the viral episode was born.

The two were seen having great chemistry, and the duo even had an interaction during Lil Yachty’s interview with Bobbi, when Drake FaceTimed the rapper during his interview.

On the FaceTime with Lil Yachty, the God’s Plan rapper joked that he’d ‘accidentally’ deleted all their footage from his interview, but now the interview has been removed from Bobbi’s YouTube for real.

The two have also unfollowed each other on Instagram, and all promo of the interview, which Bobbi ‘missed her daughter’s first birthday’ for, seems to have disappeared from the influencer‘s social media accounts.

GRV Media and Celebrity Tidbit have contacted representatives of Bobbi and Drake for comment.

Bobbi attends Drake concert before unfollowing

The news may have come as even more of a shock to fans, as Bobbi, was spotted at the Marvin’s Room singer’s concert over the weekend, as he tours North America on his massive It’s All A Blur Tour.

She took to her TikTok to share a video in her ‘awkward character’ as she can been seen standing awkwardly with her arms folded as her friends are vibing to the rapper’s music.

In the caption, Bobbi joked: “Really in my element here @ this guys concert.”

Fans loved how Althoff had attended the concert as one wrote: “Nah Bobbi and Drake are my fav duo look at how much fun she’s having!!” however, just days later, it seems the friendship may have gone south.

Fans need answers

Of course, fans have taken to Twitter to discuss the shocking news, with some devasted by the fact they didn’t get to see the full interview.

“OMG NOOOO. Just lost my appetite. Nobody dm me,” tweeted one fan upon hearing the news.

“Nooooo, This interview was so funny,” said another.

Another claimed: “And he’ll probably diss her on his song.”

“WAIT WHAT HAPPENED BETWEEN BOBBI ALTHOFF AND DRAKE???” exclaimed another.

As of yet, neither party have commented on the speculation.
