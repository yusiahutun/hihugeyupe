Cardi B and Offset Gift Daughter Kulture an Herms Birkin Bag for Her Fifth Birthday

Publish date: 2024-08-30

Cardi B and Offset threw their daughter, Kulture, the most lavish, adorable party for her fifth birthday.

For the festivities, the rappers decorated their home all pink, and dressed Kulture in a frilly Barbie-pink princess dress with a big tutu; she also wore pink lace-up flats, silver hoop earrings, and a diamond tennis bracelet. As for gifts, the five-year-old got a rare baby-pink Hermès Birkin bag.

Photos shared on Cardi's Instagram show the birthday girl sitting in front of a hot-pink piano with her new Birkin sitting on top.

"My baby is five🥹….Happy birthday to my pretty princess.It’s beautiful seeing my girl grow yet makes me a lil sad that my baby is not my little baby no more.💖🎀🍰🎂5️⃣..light lil turn up today till the weekend🎉🥳🎈," Cardi wrote in the post caption.

The "Bodak Yellow" singer also shared a video of Offset bringing out a tiny personal cake and singing happy birthday to his little girl as soon as she came out of her room in the morning. Kulture is seen smiling widely and jumping with joy.

Offset also shared a birthday tribute to his youngest daughter on Instagram, along with a photo of them together. "Happy birthday @kulturekiari your such a great daughter you make me smile every time I lay eyes on you….I can’t believe you are 5 years old already I remember holding you when you were first born in my arms….daddy’s girl I love you so much," he wrote.

Cardi and Offset married on September 20, 2017. They share two kids together, Kulture Kiari Cephus, 5, and son Wave Set Cephus, 1. Offset is also a father to sons Jordan Cephus, 13, and Kody Cephus, 8, and daughter Kalea Marie Cephus, 8, from previous relationships.
