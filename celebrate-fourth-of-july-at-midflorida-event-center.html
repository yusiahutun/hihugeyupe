Celebrate Fourth of July at MIDFLORIDA Event Center

Publish date: 2024-08-31

The MIDFLORIDA Event Center and the City of Port St. Lucie will present the Fourth of July Ultimate Experience on July 4, kicking off at 6:30 p.m.

The Ultimate Experience will feature unbeatable views of the city’s fireworks displays from exclusive, private seating on the terrace. The festivities kick off with a special DJ tribute to the Stars and Stripes. The event will also feature an interactive kids’ zone with activities, including face painting, balloon artists, and more.

Feast on a Blazin’ BBQ Buffet of pulled pork and pulled chicken smothered in barbecue sauce, baked beans, homestyle mac & cheese, coleslaw, and decadent desserts. Guests ages 21 years and older can sample the complimentary signature cocktail, The Patriot, a refreshing blend of vodka, lemonade, and blue Curaçao, topped with a splash of grenadine. A full-service cash bar will also be available.

“It’s more than just a show,” said Event Center Director Linda McCarthy. “The Ultimate Experience delivers an up-front seat to the greatest fireworks display event on the Treasure Coast. Add in the delicious buffet, air-conditioned comfort, and hours of fun for the entire family. There is simply no better way to celebrate the Fourth!”

Admission is $35 for adults and $12 for children ages 3 to 12 years. Children 2 and younger are admitted free. Tickets are on sale at the MIDFLORIDA Event Center box office, online, or by phone at (800) 514-3849. Sales tax and ticket fees apply.
