Celebrities with unexpected tooth gems: Hailey Bieber, Dua Lipa, Katy Perry and more

Publish date: 2024-04-17

Fashion is cyclical by nature, so certain trends are always bound to resurface. One style statement that has gripped the celebrity sphere is the not-so-humble tooth gem, the glitziest accessory around. The nineties-born fang fad is the ultimate must-have for It-girls and boys alike, with A-listers across the globe toying with tooth adornment.

The demand for dental bling has been catalysed by names such as Bella Hadid, Kendall Jenner and Hailey Bieber, all of whom have bejeweled their smile. The temporary gems are a painless extension to one's beauty routine, and we're getting in line to up our orthodontal game.

Discover which stars pioneered dental dazzle with tooth gems below…

Hailey Bieber

Kicking off the list is self-professed 'nepo baby' Hailey Bieber. Given her husband Justin Bieber's penchant for edgy attire spanning tattoos and piercings, it's really no surprise that Hailey has followed suit. The star has toyed with tooth gems on several occasions, adding a pop of pizzazz to her pearly whites.

Dua Lipa

Queen of cool Dua Lipa is another megastar who has dipped her toe into the world of tooth décor. The singer has previously elevated her electric wardrobe with a sprinkling of tooth gems, turning out the most fantastical fairy bread aesthetic.

Bella Hadid

Bella Hadid can do no wrong in our eyes, so if she says yes to a tooth gem, so do we. The supermodel graced the scene at fashion week last year rocking a tooth gem that infused her runway walk with some edgy attitude.

Kendall Jenner

Following in the footsteps of close pals Hailey and Bella, model Kendall Jenner also made a case for opulence orthodontics with a semi-permanent tooth gem as she partied with famous friends.

Iris Law

Whether jetting off to tropical, undisclosed locations or kicking back on set, Iris Law does so in style. The boundary-pushing daughter of actor Jude Law is the ultimate trendsetter with her avant-garde aesthetic, which includes an array of glittering tooth gems.

Adwoa Aboah

It-Brit Adowa Aboah oozes cool, and what does that mean? She's a fan of tooth gems, of course. The model adopted a Chanel logo-shaped gem as her signature dental look and glue has never looked so good.

Jaden Smith

Gems aren't just for the gals as demonstrated by Jaden Smith. The actor hit the Met Gala in true Smith style, flashing a glimpse of his grill for all to see.

Katy Perry

Wild hairstyles and out-there ensembles, it only seems natural that tooth gems should be added to Katy Perry's list of style lessons. The singer lit up the red carpet back in 2017 with a subtle twinkle of a tooth jewel.

P!nk

The ever-edgy P!nk opted for a simple solo tooth gem that was stylishly affixed to her perfect gnashers. While most eyes were fixated on her forever-changing hairstyles and punkish clothing, we couldn't help but obsess over her dental bling.

Cruz Beckham

Victoria Beckham is renowned for her sophisticated sense of style, yet her youngest son Cruz coolly shelved his mother's conventional taste. The 18-year-old showcased a gleaming metal grill as he appeared on the cover of iD Magazine and the night before his brother Brooklyn's 2021 wedding. Talk about shaking up wedding guest attire.

FKA Twigs

Last but not least is the master of the tooth gem, FKA Twigs. The adored singer-songwriter is the OG tooth gem advocate and regularly showcases whimsical dental designs that prove that tooth trends are here to stay.
