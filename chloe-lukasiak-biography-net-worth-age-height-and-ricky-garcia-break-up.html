Chloe Lukasiak Biography (Age)

The actress was born on 25 May 2001 in Churchill, Pennsylvania, as Chloe Elizabeth Lukasiak. The irony about the dancer is that after she was born, her father decided to name her after Chloe Dancer. She was raised with her younger sister Clara in Pennsylvania by her parents, Marc Lukasiak, who works at the Children’s Hospital in Pittsburgh, and her mother, Christi Lukasiak.

Chloe Lukasiak appeared as a dancer when she was only two years old. At this time she started taking dance lessons at the Abby Lee Dance. She expanded her dancing skills and was soon able to perform in music theatres.

The dancer who started the anti-bullying campaign #NobodyisYOU attended her high school online.

Professional Career

At the age of 10, Chloe made it into the Dance Moms cast when she performed with her mother and Abby Lee Miller, who owns Abby Lee Dance. The dancer stayed with the show until 2011 when her mother decided that it was not healthy for her to continue due to a comment made by Abby Lee Miller against the singer’s eyes, who is said to have caused her health problems.

After she left, she decided to continue her dance career with her YouTube channel. The channel, which it launched in 2010, had over 1.2 million subscribers and almost 200 million viewers by mid-2018. Chloe has also appeared in several music videos, including Forever In Your Mind’s Wrapped Up For Christmas and Alice by Binaca Ryan.

She made her debut as an actress in 2017 with the role of A Cowgirl’s Story, after which she got a role in Loophole. Next, she became part of the short film “Beautiful Scars”. 2018 she worked on F.R.E.D.I, Sunday morning, and the embassy. She also has a web series, Chloe Does It, which she started in 2017.

As a writer, she published her first book entitled Girl On Pointe: Chloe’s Guide to Taking Over the World in 2018.

She has received a number of awards in her career, such as the Teen Choice Awards in 2015 when she was selected for the Choice Dancer. In the same year, she also won the Industry Dance Awards for the Dancer’s Choice Awards 2015 – Favorite Dancer 17 & Under.

The star of Dance Mom had a relationship with the member of Forever in Your Mind, Ricky Garcia, who was born on 22 January 1999. The two met at the Radio Disney Music Awards in 2015, and they soon became a topic of discussion.
