Score the Naked Shoe Style Amal Clooney and Jennifer Lopez Wear for $24

Publish date: 2024-10-05

Style basics are essential to any well-functioning wardrobe: Everyone needs a classic white tee, you can’t forget about staple blue jeans, and don’t even think about tossing your denim jacket. But when thinking about your perfect capsule wardrobe, naked heels don’t usually come to mind, though they should be at the top of everyone’s list — and they’re currently as low as $24 through today while Amazon Prime’s Big Deal Days is ongoing. 

6 Best On-Sale Naked Heels on Amazon 

Naked heels, aka PVC pumps, are the new do-it-all must-haves, and even celebrities are jumping on board the trend. Amal Clooney has reached for the barely there shoes not once but twice, showing us how mesmerizing and classy they are. Jennifer Lopez followed suit, slipping on a strappy pair that gave the illusion of invisible shoes, while Hailey Bieber perfectly executed a metallic pair. And we can’t forget about Jennifer Aniston, who tried her own hand — err, foot? — at the sexy trend.    

Zriey Chunky Clear Block Heels

This sudden influx in popularity is certainly due to the style’s practicality. Usually featuring a neutral-toned base and a transparent upper, the footwear pairs seamlessly with just about everything in your wardrobe. Because of the lack of color, they can be worn throughout all four seasons, while blending in with your unique skin tone. Zriey’s chunky block heels are a perfect example of this. Featuring a beige sole, 3-inch heel, peep-toe front, and caged ankle strap, you can add an uncomplicated yet elegant shoe to your collection for 30 percent off.  

Steve Madden Liliah Sandals

Clear heels also have the potential to be very comfortable — I would know since I had to snag a last minute pair from Zara on a recent vacation. The PVC was flexible while dancing, and the materials were lightweight, minimizing foot fatigue. Steve Madden’s Lilah Sandals should have a similar comfort level, being that they include a short, chunky heel and thin double straps. One reviewer even said she wore the shoes “with a sprained ankle and brace on,” yet they still felt comfortable while dancing at a wedding.

Jessica Simpson Jaisey Rhinestone Slingback Heels

There are additionally a wide range of styles when it comes to clear heels. Sure, you can stick to clean and simple silhouettes, but don’t shy away from fun versions, such as Jessica Simpson’s Jaisey open-toe heel. This pick is sprinkled with gleaming rhinestones that capture the light around you, while a slingback strap keeps the shoe secure. Goiphrri’s crystal pumps follow the same theme, adding a bit of dazzle with a sunflower pendant at the toe and tent-like heels. Both of these options add a statement-making piece to your outfit that’s sure to produce more than a few compliments.    

Goiphrri Pointed-Toe Crystal Pumps

Though, my favorite part about naked heels is their ability to be dressed both up and down. Wear them with jeans and a blazer for a casual look or pair them with a gown and clutch for a black-tie event.
