Dua Lipa Matches Her Iconic Red Hair to Her Leather Miniskirt

Publish date: 0001-01-01

Certified It girl Dua Lipa has done it again. In her latest show of icon behavior, she matched her fiery-red locks to her skirt.

It was another day, another photo dump from the Instagram queen, and in the carousel of images she posted this morning, she showed off her masterful execution of the “sandwich method.”

The term refers to matching an element from the top half of an ensemble to an element from the bottom of an ensemble—but for the “Training Season” singer’s iteration, we’re not talking about coordinating your shirt to your shoes. Lipa had something much cooler in mind: She donned an itty-bitty leather miniskirt in the same merlot-red hue as her signature wavy tresses.

Lipa kept things spicy in a black knit cardigan that featured silver Chanel-logo snap buttons, which created a sultry cutout down the bodice, and a Matrix-esque black leather trench coat. She finished off the look with stacks of gold rings and wore glowy, rosy makeup, complete with juicy, glossy lips and a shiny red manicure.

courtesy of Dua Lipa via Instagram

The rest of the slideshow featured other moments from the pop star’s trip to Spain, including a close-up selfie of Lipa drinking out of a wine glass, a snap of herself wearing a white zip-up hoodie with the title of new single “Illusion” across the front in green and red lettering, a photo of herself in a white coat mid-jump, and a couple of pics of some recent dishes she has enjoyed. She captioned the post, “disfrutando muchoooo 🇪🇸🇪🇸🇪🇸🇪🇸🇪🇸” (which translates to “enjoying a lot”).

This isn’t the first time the Barbie star has coordinated her outfit to her hair. In November, she stunned at Variety’s Power of Women event in a scarlet corset gown to go with her then-newly dyed crimson hair. In January, at the world premiere for her film Argylle, Lipa made another statement in a deep purple sequin gown that perfectly complemented her colored tresses.
