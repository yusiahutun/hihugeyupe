Dylan Christopher Minnette is just 22 years old but, already has more experience than half his age in the professional scene. Best known for playing Clay in Netflix’s 13 Reason Why Dylan is no doubt among the elite of Hollywood.

But, with fame, Dylan has also got a fair bit of controversy and rumors on his plate. Rumors of whether the actor is gay or straight are afloat on the internet. Follow in to know the truth!

Dylan Minnette Family

Dylan was born on 29 December 1996 in Evansville, Indiana, the U.S. He hails from a small family; Born to Robyn Maker-Minnette and Craig Minnette, Dylan is an only son. 

The handsome actor stands with an average height of 5 feet 8 inches (1.73 m).

Is Dylan Gay Or Has Girlfriend?

The rumors of Dylan being gay seem to be just that; rumors. However, his female fan following will be disappointed to know that the handsome actor is dating someone.

His girlfriend is an American musician, Lydia Night. The couple has not yet disclosed how and when they met. However, they have been mentioning each other on their social media since mid-2018.

Before Lydia, Dylan was in an affair with American actress and singer, Kerris Dorsey since 2014. The pair had met on the set of ‘Alexander and the Terrible, Horrible, No Good, Very Bad Day.’ Neither of them has mentioned why they split.

Dylan's Rising Career

Scouted by an agent, Dylan started commercial modeling and acting at the age of seven. Seeking more opportunities to pursue his acting career further, Dylan moved to the land of Hollywood.

At only nine years old, Dylan made his first TV appearance on ‘Nickelodeon's popular ‘Drake & Josh’ in 2005. He also had the opportunity of playing the younger version the iconic role ‘Charlie Harper’ in ‘Two And a Half Men’ the same year.

Dylan's big break came in 2007. He secured the leading role of ‘Clay Norman’ in the TV series ‘Saving Grace’. The series lasted for three years till 2010 putting Dylan on the spotlight.

He, then, bagged a lot more roles in TV shows and movies.

Dylan Christopher Minnette features in 13 Reasons Why poster (Photo: Imdb.com)

His most notable moment in the industry came with Netflix’s 13 Reason Why.

Dylan plays Clay, a main lead in the show. As if the actor was already not on the spotlight, 13 Reasons Why skyrocketed the actor to newer heights. With Netflix renewing the show for a third season, set to release later in 2019, Dylan will no doubt be a prominent giant in Hollywood.

Even at a young age, the actor has built a strong foundation for himself with all the work he has done. He reportedly earns about $900K with an overall net worth of $3 million.

Besides acting, the 22-year-old is a guitarist and vocalist in an alternative American rock band ‘Wallows’. The band will perform at the 2019 Shaky Knees Music Festival on 3-5 May 2019.
