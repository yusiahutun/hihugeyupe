Eminem's daughter who he'd sing about in his famous lyrics is all grown up now

Publish date: 2024-04-17

Eminem has had a hugely successful music career spanning well over two decades. His daughter, Hailie Jade Scott has been a huge inspiration for his music, some could say a muse. He’s rapped about her in many songs, starting with 97 Bonnie and Clyde in 1998. Since then he’s referenced her in over 20 songs, including Hailie’s Song, My Dad’s Gone Crazy, and Cleaning Out My Closet, to name a few.

When the music icon rose to prominence in 1999, his oldest child Hailey was just four years old. However, she’s now 27 years old and is now an influencer and podcaster. The two recently had a father-and-daughter trip out at the Detroit Lions game, so let’s take a look at her now.

Eminem’s daughter Hailie Jade is all grown up

Taking to her Instagram, Hailie also posted a picture of herself at the NFL game attended with her father. In the caption, she penned: “football, family & seemingly ranch.”

Not only does she look all grown up, Hailie is actually now engaged. In February 2023, her long-term boyfriend, Evan McClintock, popped the question. And of course, she said yes.

The couple has been together for six years, after meeting at Michigan State University in 2016.

Asking Eminem for his daughter’s hand in marriage may be a little intimidating, and Evan actually revealed how he asked the Mockingbird singer.

On his girlfriend’s Just A Little Shady podcast, McClintock revealed he asked for permission to propose to Hailie at her birthday party in December 2022.

Explaining what happened during the nerve-wracking time, the 28-year-old said: “I saw your dad go downstairs, and I’m like, ‘I gotta do it right now, or I’m not doing it today, and I’m gonna have to schedule another time.'”

“So I just followed him downstairs, and, thankfully, he was just down there getting your cake and made it happen.”

The rapper has two other children

Hailie isn’t the rapper’s only child. In fact, he has two more daughters, Alina Scott and Stevie Laine Scott. Hailie is the star’s only biological child, who he shares with Kim Mathers, and was born on Christmas day. However, Marshall and Kim split in 2001, and again in 2006.

Alina was born to Kim’s sister in 1993, but was adopted by the Slim Shady star in the early 2000s.

Kim also gave birth to Stevie in between her marriages with Marshall, with her then-boyfriend, Eric Hartter. When the couple reunited in 2006, the now 50-year-old adopted Stevie.
