Erin and Ben Co. Laurel Mercantile

Publish date: 2024-07-22

If there is one thing that Erin Napier is known for when it comes to home design, it’s selecting the perfect wallpaper. She always finds the most amazing wallpaper that perfectly suits the spaces, giving the walls the color and texture needed to really tie a room together. 

And now, you can have Erin’s work on your own walls!

The Erin and Ben Co. collection is filled with beautiful florals, vintage botanicals, and whimsical folk-art inspirations, lending a personal touch to help build a layered, nostalgic home design.

“Every design is an homage to heirloom wallpaper patterns of the early 20th century in multiple modern colorways for each that I think you’re going to love” - Erin

The collection features 8 different designs with multiple colorways; choosing a favorite is almost impossible. There’s also a large-scale mural option that would be perfect for an entryway! 

Every roll and pattern is Premium Peel & Stick Wallpaper and is proudly made in the USA - just the way we like it!

I don’t know about you, but I am currently picturing how each of these patterns would look in my home! 

You can order your wallpaper today through the online store. Here is a handy measurement calculator so you can know just how much you need! 

We’ve also written out instructions for how to properly install Peel and Stick Wallpaper - a definite must-read.

This was a long-time in the making and we are so excited to offer another American-made product that will help you truly love and cherish your home.
