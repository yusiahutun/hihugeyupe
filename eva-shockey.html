Eva Shockey- Biography

Eva Shockey is a well-known Canadian writer, show host, and businesswoman. She is best known as the daughter of renowned adventurer Jim Shockey. Shockey is a well-known Canadian author, entrepreneur, TV show host, and social media personality. She joins her father, Jim Shockey, on his journey to explore and hunt the world. They both co-host the show Jim Shockey’s Hunting Adventures on the Outdoor Channel in the United States.

Shockey, like her father before her, is an excellent hunter and explorer. So far in her career, she has done commendable mountaineering and shows hosting. The hunter also runs a blog and wrote the book Taking Aim: Daring to Be Different, Happier, and Healthier in the Great Outdoors.

She is a social media influencer with one million Facebook fans, 558 thousand Instagram followers, and 76.5 thousand Twitter followers. Shockey is a new business owner who manages her website. She sells kitchen, dining, and home decor accessories, as well as jewelry and digital ornaments. Aside from her business, the explorer has worked with well-known brands such as MTN OPS, Mossy Oak, and HunterCourse.

She has appeared in a variety of media outlets, including the cover of Field & Stream magazine. Shockey received the Outdoor Channel’s Golden Moose Award for her achievements in adventure. She has also received the Outdoor Sportsman Group’s Outdoor Sportsman Award.
