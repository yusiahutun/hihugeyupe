Everleigh Rose's father passed away from FENTANYL overdose, police report reveals

Publish date: 2024-07-19

A police report have revealed that tween influencer Everleigh Rose's late father, Tommy Smith, passed away of an accidental fentanyl overdose, five months after his sudden death at age 29 sent shockwaves around the internet.

Everleigh's mother, Savannah LaBrant, 29, first confirmed the devastating news via Instagram on September 14, while asking her followers to 'pray' for her 10-year-old daughter amid the tragic loss. 

Now, the Orange County Sheriff-Coroner said that Tommy died of an accidental overdose due to acute fentanyl intoxication to People magazine, adding that while the cause of death has been confirmed, the case still remains open.

Savannah dated Tommy from 2012 to 2016, and she gave birth to Everleigh in December 2012.

Fentanyl is a synthetic opioid that is 80-100 times stronger than morphine and 50 times stronger than heroine. 

It was created to treat cancer but is often taken for recreational use, as well as added to drugs like cocaine or heroin to increase their potency or make them more profitable.

According to DrugAbuseStatstics.org, less than 0.07 ounces of fentanyl causes certain death, and the drug is a factor in 53 per cent of drug overdoses.
