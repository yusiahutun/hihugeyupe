All the Glamorous Looks from the Oscar Nominees Luncheon

Publish date: 2024-04-30
1

Kristen Stewart

Momodu Mansaray//Getty Images

Stewart repped Chanel at the luncheon, wearing a black cropped cardigan with silver sleeve embellishments and a pair of black trousers.

2

Penélope Cruz

Neilson Barnard//Getty Images

Cruz wore a bright-pink bouclé minidress from Chanel's spring 2022 collection and strappy black heels.

3

Ariana DeBose

Neilson Barnard//Getty Images

The West Side Story actress wore a salmon-pink, double-breasted suit from Hugo Boss, as well as strappy embellished heels.

4

Billie Eilish

Momodu Mansaray//Getty Images

The Oscar-nominated singer wore a velvet button-up and a netted lace midi skirt from Gucci. She capped off the look with black platforms.

5

Finneas O'Connell and Claudia Sulewski

Neilson Barnard//Getty Images

O'Connell wore an olive pinstriped suit with a red button-up and matching red platforms. Meanwhile, Sulewski wore a champagne slip with abdominal cutouts and mint-green sandals.

6

Will Smith and Aunjanue Ellis

Momodu Mansaray//Getty Images

Smith wore a blue checkered double-breasted suit with a caramel turtleneck and brown leather boots. Ellis wore a patterned hot-pink suit with black fur trim on the lapels, along with a black bow tie and black pumps.

7

Jessica Chastain

Momodu Mansaray//Getty Images

Chastain wore a black silk faille long-sleeved midi dress from Gucci, as well as a pair of black platform heels.

8

Andrew Garfield

Maggie Gyllenhaal

10

Sian Heder

VALERIE MACON//Getty Images

The CODA director and writer wore a pleated orange dress featuring a pussy bow collar. She also carried a baby-pink clutch and wore strappy cream-colored heels.
