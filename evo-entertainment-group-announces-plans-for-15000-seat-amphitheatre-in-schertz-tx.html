EVO Entertainment Group Announces Plans for 15,000 Seat Amphitheatre in Schertz, TX

Publish date: 2024-10-05

By 5G Studios, Dallas | Property of EVO Entertainment Group® (PRNewsfoto/EVO Entertainment Group)

SAN MARCOS, TX (CelebrityAccess) – EVO Entertainment Group have announced plans for their newest entertainment destination, a 15,000 seat Amphitheatre called EVO Live located in Schertz, Texas.

The massive 150,000 square foot venue will sit on 27 acres directly behind EVO’s previously announced cinema-entertainment center at The Ranch at Old Wiederstein, which is currently under construction.

When complete, the state-of-the-art live music venue will offer multiple tiers of covered & uncovered seating, multiple food & beverage options, private clubs & viewing areas, and a massive stage which will host global talent as well as local community events.

“Music & Live Entertainment has been a passion of mine for my entire life, and something I’ve dreamt of incorporating into the EVO vision from the start,” says EVO Entertainment Group CEO, Mitch Roberts.

“The 35-corridor, particularly the Schertz area, is absolutely exploding but has a noticeable void when it comes to entertainment options. EVO, EVO Live & entire Ranch at Old Weiderstein development are primed to not only fill that void but become a truly incredible entertainment destination unlike anything we’re currently seeing in Central Texas. This will be a landmark project not only for our company, but for the city of Schertz and the surrounding communities as well.”

Together, EVO’s two projects at The Ranch at Old Weiderstein are expected to draw upwards of two-million guests to Schertz. No official opening date has been set, however, the EVO Live project is estimated to open at some point next year while EVO’s next generation Cinema-Entertainment Center is on target to open in January of 2019.
