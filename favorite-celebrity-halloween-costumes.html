75 Cool Halloween Costumes - Best Celeb Halloween Costume Ideas

Publish date: 2024-08-30

Every product on this page was chosen by a Harper's BAZAAR editor. We may earn commission on some of the items you choose to buy.

1

The Classic Witch

Johnny Nunez//Getty Images

Follow Cardi B's lead this October and opt for a sultry take on the classic witch costume. Just wear your favorite LBD with long gloves and, of course, the pointed hat to top it all off. To re-create the rapper's cinched look, add a matching corset.

Shop the look: Dion Lee Lattice Strap Corset

2

Alice from 'Closer'

Gotham//Getty Images

Model Elsa Hosk channeled Natalie Portman's character, Alice, in the 2004 romantic drama Closer. Key pieces in successfully pulling off the look include a fringed bra top, platform pleaser sandals, and, of course, the pink wig.

Shop the look: Fleur du Mal Fringe Ouvert Bra

3

1960s Fembot

Bryan Steffy//Getty Images

Back in 2018, Kendall Jenner went full-on Fembot à la movie series Austin Powers, and we'll admit it's a pretty genius idea. For those who own a pastel-colored negligee with fur trimmings, you're already halfway there.

Shop the look: Gilda & Pearl Diana Sheer Slip Dress

4

Mod Stewardess

Gabe Ginsberg//Getty Images

Iconic supermodel Cindy Crawford, along with husband Rande Gerber and Hollywood actor George Clooney, decided to go the open-skies route, dressing up as pilots and a flight attendant. White boots and a sleeveless shift dress will emulate the distinct mod vibe.

Shop the look: Nomasei Stretch Boots

5

Bloody Baseball Player

NBC//Getty Images

Kristen Stewart sported a slightly gory take on the baseball player costume by adding fake blood to her crewneck top and pinstriped pants combo. And, of course, don't forget the dad cap to, quite literally, top it all off.

Shop the look: Veronica Beard Jeans Mason Baseball Tee

6

Plastic Mermaid

Astrid Stawiarz//Getty Images

A satirical take on the classic mermaid costume, Alexa Chung's 2018 Halloween look includes the obvious long sequined skirt and bra top comb, but with a plastic bag addition. This is a nod to—one can only assume—the global crisis of ocean pollution.

Shop the look: Lapointe Sequined Slit Skirt

Advertisement - Continue Reading Below

7

Miss Sara Bellum from 'The Powerpuff Girls'

Robin L Marshall//Getty Images

Leave it to Doja Cat to take a character from everyone's favorite nostalgic cartoon show and turn it into a must-wear costume for Halloween. The singer belted a plunging red blazer dress to re-create the elusive look of Miss Sara Bellum from The Powerpuff Girls.

Shop the look: Cazinc the Label Blazer Dress

8

Elton "Rocketman" John

Kevin Mazur//Getty Images

Harry Styles channeled a quintessential Elton John look, featuring a glittered Dodgers baseball ensemble and oversized embellished sunglasses. Of course, Gucci has similar styles should you also want to emulate Rocketman this Halloween.

Shop the look: Gucci Diamante-Embellished Sunglasses

9

Peik Lin from 'Crazy Rich Asians'

JB Lacroix//Getty Images

Olivia Munn took a rather niche approach back in 2018, dressing up as Awkwafina's Crazy Rich Asians character, Peik Lin. The A-lister just layered bold prints with statement accessories and a blonde wig—et voila.

Shop the look: Rowing Blazers Warm & Wonderful Sheep Vest

Advertisement - Continue Reading Below

10

Dionne Davenport from 'Clueless'

Craig Barritt//Getty Images

In a checked skirt suit, Lupita Nyong'o dressed up as '90s style icon Dionne Davenport from the classic teen flick Clueless. Get the look with an oversized plaid blazer layered over a fitted cardigan.

Shop the look: Everlane '80s Blazer

11

Latex Goddess

Noam Galai//Getty Images

Top model Duckie Thot donned a floor-length latex look with pierced details on the "black carpet" of Heidi Klum's famed Halloween event. For a milder take, consider a bodysuit or cutout, fitted dress over latex pants instead.

Shop the look: DieselP-Arcy Pants

12

Flo-Jo

Taylor Hill//Getty Images

Model and actress Liris Crosse emulated Olympic track star Florence "Flo-Jo" Griffith Joyner with a brightly colored, one-leg catsuit and long red acrylic nails. Get the look with an equally bold one-piece layered over leggings.

Shop the look: SKIMS Metallic Swim Cycle Suit

Advertisement - Continue Reading Below

13

Skeleton Queen

Ricky Vigil M//Getty Images

A few years ago in London, Maya Jama offered up a more ambiguous route to the classic skeleton costume with a lamé slipdress and coordinating face paint. The one-step ensemble makes for a great last-minute look (cape optional).

Shop the look: Rick Owens Silver Slip Dress

14

The Players Club

Gilbert Carrasquillo//Getty Images

Model Winnie Harlow attended Heidi Klum's 19th annual Halloween party in a blonde wig and a red latex bodysuit with matching thigh-high boots. Channeling Ronnie from The Players Club, the costume included fake dollar bills dragging at the hem of her faux fur coat as a nice finishing touch.

Shop the look: Anoeses "Stella010" Latex Bodysuit

15

Cruella De Vil

Gareth Cattermole//Getty Images

With just one glance, it's apparent that Iggy Azalea is dressed up as everyone's favorite Disney villain, Cruella De Vil. Apart from the real-life Dalmatian on the red carpet, the black-and-white look and bold red lip are dead giveaways.

Shop the look: Apparis Milly Jacket

Advertisement - Continue Reading Below

16

Black Swan

Gilbert Carrasquillo//Getty Images

If you're not sure what to wear this Halloween season, you can't go wrong with some iteration of the Black Swan costume. Easy to style yet just niche enough to not be too common, the look can be made more laid-back by swapping the skirt and stockings for leather pants.

Shop the look: Nadine Merabi Roxy Black Skirt

17

Dorothy and the Tin Man

Jim Spellman//Getty Images

A cute couple's costume idea, The Wizard of Oz's characters are actually quite easy to achieve. Thanks to brands like Hill House and LoveShackFancy, gingham smock dresses are as popular as ever, while the key to nailing Ben Platt's Tin Man is an all-silver look.

Shop the look: LoveShackFancy Chennedy Mini Dress

18

'80s Rocker

Craig Barritt//Getty Images

Fully committing to the '80s rocker vibe, the iconic Mariah Carey wore a spiked blonde wig with leather hot pants and a neon electric guitar. Re-create the look for yourself by throwing a classic biker jacket over a distressed band tee. (Bonus points if you go for the thigh-high boots.)

Shop the look: Deadwood Joan Leather Biker Jacket

Advertisement - Continue Reading Below

19

Maleficent

Gotham//Getty Images

Disney's Maleficent costume in theory can be difficult to execute. But in reality, all you need is a hooded cape with a horned headband for the full effect. Add a smoky eye and burgundy lip for extra drama.

Shop the look: RTA Jude Poncho Dress

20

Pinup Baby

Gilbert Carrasquillo//Getty Images

Gizele Oliveira was yet another guest at Heidi Klum's beloved Halloween bash. The model attended the party in her best "pinup baby" costume with a baker boy cap, bustier and high-rise disco pants for the ultimate 1960s look.

Shop the look: Ruslan Baginskiy Beaded Cotton Cap
