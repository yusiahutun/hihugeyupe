Hailey Bieber Looks So Chic in an Oversized Tan Coat and Tiny Leather Shorts

Publish date: 2024-07-10

Hailey Bieber and Saint Laurent is always a match made in heaven.

On Thursday, the model wore a super chic spin on one of her signature style pairings, courtesy of the French luxury house, In the pics, she wears a luxurious, oversized wool coat, in a warm tan shade, over black lambskin micro-shorts and a taupe, off-the-shoulder bodysuit with long sleeves and ruched detailing. She finished the look with sheer black tights, black pointed-toe pumps, gold stud earrings, and thin oval shades.

As for her glam look, the style star wore her hair pulled back in a slick side-part bun, with a "glazed donut" complexion and a pink lip.

Saint Laurent

Saint Laurent

The oversized coat and tiny bottoms combo is one of the model's go-to looks, and she's been spotted in variations ranging from cool leather jackets over tiny black miniskirts, to a Matrix-esque leather trench over matching hot shorts. Last January, Hailey wore a preppier spin on the pairing during an NYC date with her husband Justin Bieber, where she layered a cozy black trench over a preppy gray pleated miniskirt, a beige V-neck sweater, and a white tee.

For her footwear, she opted for chunky black loafers with long white socks and black sheer tights from Calzedonia. Her accessories included a quilted pale yellow shoulder bag and tiny black sunglasses with gold frames.

Gotham//Getty Images

The Rhode founder had a busy week in Toronto, where she also celebrated the Canada launch of her skincare line. For the occasion, the model partied in an icy blue ensemble pulled straight from LaQuan Smith's fall 2023 runway, comprised of an asymmetrical miniskirt, a narrow bandeau bra, and a boxy overcoat with black lining.

She completed the look with sheer Calzedonia tights and a pair of chunky gold earrings, and wore her hair down in a wavy side part. Her sultry makeup look included a dark brown eye shadow and a deep pink glossy lip.
