Heather Graham, 53, joins boyfriend John de Neufville, 46,on a romantic dinner date

Publish date: 2024-09-29

Heather Graham enjoyed a romantic dinner date with boyfriend John de Neufville in Santa Monica on Tuesday night.

The actress, 53, held hands with her snowboarder beau, 46, as they left celebrity favourite, Italian restaurant, Giorgio Baldi.

Heather put on a leggy display in a tiger print mini dress that she paired with towering black heels.

Wearing her blonde locks loose in a straight style, Heather kept her makeup simple, opting for a light red lipstick.

Meanwhile, John looked dapper in a white shirt and jeans and complemented his outfit with a blue blazer and camouflage print trainers. 

Beauty: Heather Graham put on a leggy display in a tiger print mini dress as she left a romantic dinner date in Santa Monica with boyfriend John de Neufville on Tuesday night 

Heather and John smiled and looked relaxed and happy after their quality time together in Santa Monica. 

The couple have been enjoying lots of sweet dates during their relationship, including a bike ride in New York City in September. 

They also took time out to visit Giorgio Baldi for dinner in September.

And they managed to cram in a holiday to their jam-packed schedule earlier that month in the Amalfi Coast after attending the Venice Film Festival.  

The Hollywood beauty shared images of their stunning Italian getaway on Instagram, which included luxury boat rides and swoon-worthy swims in the ocean.

The Austin Powers star famously dated the late actor Heath Ledger between 2000 and 2001 and has also been linked to other stars such as Matthew Perry, Chris Weltz, and Tommy Alastra.

Heather's latest love holds the world record for the most vertical feet ever snowboarded in a year, which he accomplished in 2014 by snowboarding on every continent.  

It comes after Heather voiced her enthusiasm for the release of a currently-untitled erotic horror project, in which she is set to appear, with a post that was shared to her Instagram account.

Glam: She paired her glamorous look with black heels as they left celebrity favourite Italian restaurant Giorgio Baldi

The actress shared a shot of a Deadline article to her account, and penned a short message where she expressed that she had 'so much fun working on this movie.'

The performer went on to write that she enjoyed filling what she described as a 'super interesting challenging role.'

According to the media outlet, the movie is partially based on H.P. Lovecraft's short story The Thing On The Doorstep.

The movie will be centered on a psychiatrist, played by Graham, who becomes fixated on one of her patients who happens to have multiple personalities.

Letting everyone know: It comes after Heather last year voiced her enthusiasm for the release of a currently-untitled erotic horror project in an Instagram post (pictured)

Other cast members in the upcoming feature include Johnathon Schaech and Bruce Davison, among others.

Physical production on the feature commenced in 2022 and Mississippi served as a primary filming location.

Producer Barbara Crampton, who will also star in the feature, gave a statement to Deadline to praise Graham's acting ability. 

'I was ecstatic when...Heather Graham signed on – her natural charm is always in evidence, but we get to see a different untapped side of her abilities here,' she stated.

Director Joe Lynch added that he was happy to have been able to collaborate with the actress and her castmates.

He stated: 'Working with Amp and such a fantastic cast – including Heather, Judah, Barbara, Bruce and Johnathon – we're so excited that this sexy, funny, scary film isn't just a modernized love letter to Lovecraft, but the kind of provocatively entertaining 'sin-ema' I believe audiences are salivating for!'

The currently-untitled horror feature's forthcoming release date has not been announced as of yet.
