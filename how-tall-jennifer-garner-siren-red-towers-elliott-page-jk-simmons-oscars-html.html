How tall is Jennifer Garner? The siren in red towers over Elliott Page and JK Simmons at the Oscars

Publish date: 2024-07-09

Jennifer Garner was a bombshell in a red gown as she stepped on the stage of the 94th annual Academy Awards at the Dolby Theater in Hollywood on Sunday.

But once the Alias vet posed next to Elliott Page and JK Simmons, her Juno co-stars, she looked to be incredibly tall.

That's because the ex-wife of Ben Affleck is on the taller side at 5ft8in - and most likely 6ftin in her red platform high heels by Roger Vivier. Page is 5ft1in and JK is 5ft10in.

Garner glowed in her off the shoulder gown.

She also donned a pair of perfectly matched open-toe heels that gave her extra height.

She had her dark brown tresses styled long and flowing to just above the middle of her back, with some natural waves and a part off to the right.

The leading lady decided to keep the accessories to a minimum, wearing a pair of dangling earrings, a sparkly bracelet, and a couple of rings. 

And of course, Garner held the attention of admirers with her trademark perky smile and personality. 

This was Garner's first appearance at Hollywood's biggest night since the 2018 ceremony, according to Entertainment Tonight.

After moving to a new location last year - Union Station in Downtown Los Angeles - Sunday's Oscars are airing from the show's longtime venue of the Dolby Theatre.  

Regina Hall, Amy Schumer and Wanda Sykes co-hosted the ceremony, which comes on the heels of the past three shows that were entirely hostless.   

This was the first time since the 83rd Academy Awards in 2011 where the ceremony featured multiple hosts.
