How to Get More Prize Drop Chips: Monopoly Go

Publish date: 2024-03-08

Get as many Prize Drop Chips as you want!

Monopoly Go facilitates you to win rewards in a variety of ways. You can earn a hefty amount of rewards from the Events that keep appearing every now and then. You can also earn many rewards playing the Peg-E minigame in Monopoly Go. This Plinko-styled minigame runs for a few days almost every week.

In order to play the game, you will need prize drop chips. You will have to drop these chips into the board and hit any of the bumpers, which will then drop to the reward zone. As this minigame stays for only a day or two, you will have to collect the chips fast. Also, if you want to earn a lot, you will need plenty of these prize drop chips. So, without further ado, delve into the guide about how to get more prize drop chips in Monopoly Go.

How to Get More Prize Drop Chips in Monopoly Go

The Easiest way to collect Prize Drop Chips in Monopoly Go is by rolling the dice. The gameplay will give you Prize Drop Chips here and there. But following this way will only give you much. Also, you are not sure if you will earn Prize Drop Chips or not after your dice roll.

The best way to collect more Prize Drop Chips is through Events that take place in Monopoly Go every once in a while. By completing the objectives or landing on some specific places, you will earn points. And through those points, you can redeem Prize Drop Chips.

For example, you could achieve 30 Prize Drop Chips using 60 points in the Pumpkin Prowl event. So, the moment you see a new event added, you should earn as many points as you can to redeem large amounts of Prize Drop Chips. One thing to note is that the unused chips will vanish once the minigame ends.
