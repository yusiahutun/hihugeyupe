Career and Progression

Ingrid started doing modeling and acting at the age of twenty appearing in 16 telenovelas. Afterward, in 1985, she moved to Miami, Florida and opened a high fashion luxury boutique’ La Capricieuse’ in Miami’s Coconut Grove.

Afterward, in January 1993, Ingrid opened a restaurant with her mother and two business partners. Ingrid got the inspiration of cooking from her mother who is also a trained chef. The restaurant soon became famous as the first restaurant in Miami with the feature of tabletop cooking on heated lava rocks.

In 2002 as she hosted a cooking segment on a Miami- area television show, she was offered a bi-weekly segment on the Spanish language morning show, ‘ Despierta America.’ In 2005, she launched her own show ‘ Delicioso’ on Direct TV. The show now comes on Galavision.  Her another show ‘Simply Delicioso’ also runs on Food Network from July 2007.

Ingrid has also tried her hand in writing; her cookbook, ‘Simply Delicioso: A Collection of Everyday Recipes with a Latin Twist,’ was published on February 8, 2008, by Clarkson Potter.
