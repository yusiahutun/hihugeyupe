Jennifer Lawrence Bucks Gold Gown Trend, Embraces Tom Ford

Publish date: 2024-07-18

There's the glimmer of a gilded shoe and some shiny piping along the sexy open back, but Hunger Games star, Jennifer Lawrence, dropped the sweeping gold gown habit she seemed to be forming on her press tour — she's worn versions by both Prabal Gurung and Ralph Lauren — in favor of something a lot less lamé at the film's latest premiere in Paris. The young starlet proved she can demure with the best of them in a gorgeous black Tom Ford sheath we didn't even see coming — probably because Ford keeps his collections under wraps until they're available for purchase. We adore a good surprise.
