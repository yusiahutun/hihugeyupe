Joe Costner: Age, Wiki, Biography

Publish date: 2024-08-05

Born to the famous actor Kevin Costner and internet personality Cindy Costner, Joe Costner is also an actor and music producer. Although Joe was born with a silver spoon in his mouth, he did everything to make his dream come true.

Here, we look into the life of Joe Costner. We discuss his age, biography, dating life, and much more.

Joe Costner Biography

Joe Costner was famous from when he was born. Being the child of Kevin Costner gave him an instant lift up in the film industry. He debuted as an actor in 1996, at a very young age. Joe was only 8 when he appeared in his first movie, Tin Cup (1996). He is still famous for his appearance in Tin Cup.

The actor then appeared in many other films like Better than yourself, The postman (1997).

Though Joe started his career as an actor, his true passion was music. The actor turned musician started his own record company named Spartan Recording. The actor has a very unique studio space. He remodeled his trailer into a mobile studio workspace.

The emerging actor mostly works as a music producer and audio engineer at his company. But he has not left behind acting; Joe appears in minor roles in movies. His most recent include Hawaii (2019), Devil’s life (2020).

Age and Family

Joe Tedrick Costner was born in 1988 in California, USA. His birthday falls on January 31. He is now (2022) 34 years old. The actor did his schooling in California. Joe then graduated from the University of Colorado, Boulder. His major was history.

Joe has a huge family. The actor grew up with his sisters, Annie Costner and Lily Costner. His parents- Kevin and Cindy, split up when he was only 6. Now, Joe has a half-sister named Grace Costner and three half-brothers named Liam Costner, Cayden Costner, Hayes Costner.
