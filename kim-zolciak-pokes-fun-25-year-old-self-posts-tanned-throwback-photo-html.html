Kim Zolciak pokes fun at her 25-year-old self

Publish date: 2024-06-24

She recently denied claims she's gone under the knife to have a full facelift.

But Kim Zolciak, 37, didn't shy away from throwing it back on Thursday and bravely shared a photo of herself before she became rich and famous.

The mother-of-six can be seen as an overly tanned and barbie-esque 25-year-old - and she wasn't afraid to poke fun at herself about it.

Meanwhile, the wife of Atlanta Falcons football player Kroy Biermann has slammed reports that she's had a full facelift.

In the past, the TV star has admitted to tummy tucks and breast implants but took to Twitter to angrily respond to RadarOnline's report which asked top plastic surgeons their opinion about her youthful-looking visage.

'Top doctors my a**! I have not had surgery on my face period!!!!' Kim tweeted Tuesday morning
