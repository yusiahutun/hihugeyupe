Kristine Leahy Wiki, Bio, Age

Kristine Leahy was born on October 16, 1986, in Chicago, Illinois. Around the time of her birth, Chicago was humming with the fame of the invincible Michael Jordan. Leahy quickly developed into a big sports fan alongside her brothers, who were also athletes themselves.

To this day, Kristine is an avid Chicago Bulls fan and idolater of NBA great Michael Jordan. Her Instagram account is filled with photos of her MJ T-shirts. Her favorite movie is MJ’s Space Jam.

Leahy is the kind of girl most men dream of being with her, her three most popular things are sports, rap, and animals. She owns a cute little dog called Jersey, which she often shows on Instagram.

Leahy took up some sport herself while attending Prairie Ridge High School in Crystal Lake, Illinois. After graduating from high school, Leahy moved to Boston, where she studied journalism with a focus on sports at Boston University.

She graduated in 2009 with a Bachelor of Science in Journalism, Concentration in Broadcasting Journalism, Minor in Sociology.

During her studies at the BU, Kristine created, produced and hosted the only BUTV sports show entitled “Offsides”. In the show, Leahy edited episodes and conducted interviews. During her time at the BU, she had the chance to interview Red Sox GM Theo Epstein.

Kristine did an internship at Fox 25 and KCAL in LA. Among her roles was the moderation of the “Lakers Fan Cam”.

After graduating in 2009, Leahy stayed in Boston to start her career. She landed a job at the Boston WEEI radio station and reported on the WEEI website for Boston’s large teams. She later became an in-area and post-game wrap-up host for Boston Celtics.

According to her LinkedIn profile, Kristine left Boston for California in 2012. She has had many successes in Cali, from the first woman to call out a horse race in 2013 to the official reporter for a number of stores.

Leahy has hosted the CBS Sports Network Lead Off and conducted mid-term interviews from the NCAA tournament.

Her first national job came in March 2015 when she replaced Jenn Brown at American Ninja Warrior. In September 2015, former ESPN radio host Colin Cowherd selected her for his buddy on his new Fox show.

Leahy also works as a presenter and reporter for KCBS-TV in Los Angeles. More details about her career can be found on her website.
