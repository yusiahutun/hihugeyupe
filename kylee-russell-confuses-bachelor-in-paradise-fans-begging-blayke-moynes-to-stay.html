Kylee Russell confuses Bachelor in Paradise fans begging Blake Moynes to stay Sat 2 December 20

Publish date: 2024-03-02

It’s hard to work out whether more tears are being shed on Bachelor in Paradise season 9 than romances are being formed. As the show’s finale looms closer, the couples have to decide whether they’re going to commit to one another and get engaged or go their separate ways. One Bachelor in Paradise star chose to leave the show early, and now fans are confused by Kylee Russell’s actions.

As the ABC show’s final episode approaches, tensions are rising on the beach in Paradise. Things go so far for wildlife manager Blake Moynes that he decides to exit the series early. Blake formed connections with Jess Girod, and Genevie Mayo during his time in Mexico. His ex, Katie Thurston, showed up on the beach during season 9, too.

Kylee from Bachelor in Paradise

Bachelor in Paradise has been a whirlwind for some of the dating show stars. And, even though there is only one episode left of the season, the drama is still amped up in episode 9.

Kylee Russell had her eye on Aven Jones before he even arrived in Paradise. So, when fans see that she’s begging Blake to stay on the show, some are confused.

Kylee and Aven were one of Paradise’s stronger couples this season. But, whether they leave the show engaged will be revealed during the December 8 finale.

Kylee begs Blake to stay

BiP episode 9 sees Blake and Jess decide that they can’t make their relationship work.

Blake decides that he wants to leave the show, and continues to do so despite Kylee’s efforts to make him stay.

Kylee chases after Blake after he heads to a car ready to escort him out of Paradise.

She says that she “can’t be here without” him and adds that it makes her “sad” that he’s leaving.

Kylee adds: “I’m not kidding, don’t go,” but Blake decides it’s best for him to leave.

Bachelor in Paradise fans are confused

Some BiP viewers appear to be very confused after Kylee and Blake’s chat.

Many are asking “what was that interaction between Blake and Kyle” and some are suggesting that they were “flirting.”

One fan commented: “She can’t do it without him, what… is she doing with Aven if she needs Blake to stay.”

Another said: “She asked him so there’s no one here you would be willing to stay for? Was she hoping he would pursue her?”

Someone else added: “I think Blake likes Kylee.”

Another fan took things a step further, writing: “I really thought Blake and Kylee were gonna kiss when she asked him to stay”

One fan tweeted on X (Twitter) that the interaction made sense to them, saying: “Why is everyone so confused about Kylee and Blake? Did yall miss them hanging out all the time ? They were always together at the beach… lol tanning”
