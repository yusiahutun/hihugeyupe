Who is Laura Jarrett? Bio, Father, Wiki

Laura Jarrett was born in 1983 and as of 2019, she is around 36 years old as per her age. She belongs to Afro-American ethnicity and holds an American nationality. She was born to William Jarrett and Valerie Jarrett in Washington DC, USA.

Jarrett mother is a businesswoman and former government official whereas, her father is a doctor and the son of Chicago Sun-Times columnist Vernon Jarrett. Her parents encouraged and motivated her to follow in their footsteps with the powerful work. Furthermore, they have since been quoted in interviews as saying,

“We hoped we would make our daughter proud.”

Unfortunately, Jarrett lost her father when she was only ten years old. She attended schools in Chicago and graduated from Harvard Law School in 2010. During her college time, she worked on the Harvard Journal of Law and Gender as a technical writer, editor and article editor.

Jarrett started her career as a judicial law clerk for the Honorable Rebecca Pallmeyer in Northern District of Illinois, and later for Honorable Ann C. Williams on the Seventh Circuit Court of Appeal. She worked as a litigation attorney in Chicago, Illinois.

After a couple of years, Jarrett switched her career to reporting. She got appointed as a justice reporter for CNN in the year 2016. Since 2016 Jarrett is serving as a justice reporter for CNN with her location as the Washington D.C. Bureau of CNN.
