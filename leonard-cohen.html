Leonard Cohen Hollywood Life

Publish date: 2024-06-13

Bio:
Leonard Cohen (born Leonard Norman Cohen on Sept. 21, 1934 in Quebec, Canada) is a Canadian singer, songwriter, poet and author. The legendary musician was known for his many hits, including “Suzanne,” “Bird On A Wire,” and “Hallelujah.” Sadly, Leonard passed away on Nov. 10, 2016 at age 82.

Best Known For:
Leonard was known for being a famous singer/songwriter, poet and author.

Personal Life:
Leonard was born in Quebec Canada, but also spent time living in Greece and America. Leonard was never married, though he had passionate relationships with many women, including Marianne C. Stang Jensen Ihlen, Suzanne Elrod, Dominique Issermann and Rebecca De Mornay. He also has two children, Adam and Lorca. He died on Nov. 10, 2016 at 82 years old.
