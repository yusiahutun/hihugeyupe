Who is Liza Morales?

As the ex-girlfriend of basketball player and ex-Kardashian member, Lamar Odom, Liza is more than used to being a ‘Basketball Wife’.

Liza and Lamar have two children together: Destiny, 21, and Lamar Jr., 19. The ex-couple also had another son in 2006, who they sadly lost to infant death syndrome at six months.

Splitting just after the death, Lamar’s alleged infidelity has been cited as the cause on numerous occasions. For reality TV fans, this won’t be the first time you’ve met Liza Morales, as she briefly appeared in TLC’s Starter Wives Confidential in 2013. The show however was axed to low ratings.

Starter Wives Confidential focused on the ex-partners of famous people, who were there before the fame but abandoned after it came. Lamar married Khloe a year after his split with Liza, but she says he would insist on only marrying once he was retired to her.

What is Liza Morales’ net worth 2021?

According to Celebrity Net Worth, Liza Morales has a 2021 net worth of $3million, owning baby clothing line Rich Soil Baby.

Ex-boyfriend Lamar Odom’s net worth is a reported $30million. However, finances has actually been a point of disagreement for the pair.

In October, she posted a not-so-cryptic Instagram post, writing: “Let’s get to the real…where is LJ’s college money???We have been asking for it.”

It’s likely that the disagreements between Liza and Lamar will be discussed on Basketball Wives, so keep tuning in to catch it.
