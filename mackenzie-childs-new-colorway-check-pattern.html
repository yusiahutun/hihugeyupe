MacKenzie-Childs Releases New Colorway in Iconic Check Pattern

Publish date: 2024-10-03

For the first time in two years, MacKenzie-Childs is releasing a new colorway in their iconic check pattern, and this one will have pink-lovers blushing with excitement. The home décor brand announced today that the classic check pattern will now come in Rosy Check, a playful pink and white design that's sure to capture shoppers' hearts.

This is the first time the artisan brand has released a new color in the check pattern since the release of Sterling Check in 2022, a neutral gray and white design that's notably more muted than both its Courtly and Royal Check predecessors. Rosy Check is a decidedly feminine design that mixes beautifully with the brand's Flower Market and Wildflower collections.

Courtesy MacKenzie-Childs

Fans of the brand's iconic pattern who love a lighter, more spring-ready colorway will be ecstatic with the news. “Meet your new color crush! It brings us great pleasure to introduce Rosy Check, a highly requested new petal pink check colorway, to the whimsical world of MacKenzie-Childs,” said Rebecca Proctor, Chief Brand Officer and Creative Director, MacKenzie-Childs. “It’s blushing and soft, but with a visual strength powerful enough to make its own bold statement among our iconic check patterns. Rosy Check evokes joy and playfulness through a fresh color palette and is destined to find a place in every corner of your home.”

The collection includes over 20 enamel pieces, from dinnerware, serveware, and more, all hand-painted in Aurora, New York. Shoppers can expect more from the collection this fall.
