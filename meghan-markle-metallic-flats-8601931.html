Meghan Markles Ageless $1,095 Shoes Channeled Martha Stewart

Publish date: 2024-07-23

Meghan Markle has established herself as a fashion ‘It’ girl thanks to her sleek riding boots and versatile denim pieces. While royals aren’t typically known for their practical wardrobes, that’s not the case for Markle — in fact, she just wore some of the most sensible shoes I’ve seen.

Earlier this week, Markle stepped out to dinner wearing a chunky sweater, dark-wash jeans, large tote bag, and gray ballet flats that are rumored to be from Chanel and originally retail for $1,095. The metallic, cap-toe footwear instantly elevated her outfit and became the focal point of her simple ensemble. 

7 Metallic Ballet Flats

Markle’s ballet slippers nodded to Martha Stewart and Anne Hathaway’s bold shiny shoe obsession, though Katie Holmes, Sarah Jessica Parker, and Selena Gomez are also fans of the ageless trend. I’m no A-lister, but I’m a metallic shoe enthusiast, too; I dressed up my all-black New Years Eve outfit with disco ball boots and received endless compliments.

Thankfully, you don’t have to drop over $1,000 to recreate Markle’s champion footwear style. Instead, shop these seven picks, starting at $19. 

Vionic Klara Cap-Toe Flat

Vionic’s Kara flats offer a bright take on Markle’s stunning shoes and include the same cap toe, modern square front, and sweet bow detail. A dainty keyhole adds the perfect touch, while a slight heel adds comfort and height. Shop this shoe in sizes 5 to 13. 

Bandolino Edition Ballet Flat

If you’re looking for peak comfort and style, consider Bandolino’s Edition ballet flat. Sold at Amazon and Zappos, this on-sale footwear boasts a round toe, leather silhouette, padded insole, and stretchy upper, which makes taking it on and off a breeze. Wear it with English Factory’s puff-sleeve cardigan and a pair of ankle-grazing Madewell jeans for a chic look.  

Sam Edelman Felicia Luxe Flat

You can never go wrong with Sam Edelman; I own flats from the brand and they are it. These Felicia Luxe flats come in a gorgeous silver hue, while a braided bow and logo charms decorate the round toe. The slim build elongates your frame and easily pairs with jeans, skirts, and dresses. 

Amazon Essentials Belice Ballet Flat

Don’t sleep on Amazon’s number one best-selling flats that have racked up over 46,000 five-star ratings. The Markle-inspired shoes include a memory foam footbed, elastic top, smooth toe, and faux leather. For just $24, wear them to the office, happy hour, weekend brunch, dates, and more.
