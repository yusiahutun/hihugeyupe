MTV star meets biological dad and fans cant believe how much they look alike Tue 13 February

Publish date: 2024-07-11

Jersey Shore: Family Vacation gets its name from the fact Pauly D, Snooki, and co are just like a family. But, this season, it turns out there are some blood relatives the MTV stars are yet to meet. Season 7 sees Angelina Pivarnick meet her biological dad for the first time.

Angelina Pivarnick rose to reality TV fame as an original cast member of Jersey Shore back in 2009. She starred alongside JWoww, Sammi Sweetheart, Vinny Guadagnino, and the rest of the OGs before a brief break in seasons 3 and 4. Angelina returned as a guest during seasons 5 and 6 and is now a Jersey Shore: Family Vacation cast member.

Jersey Shore’s Angelina meets her dad

As if Sammi Sweetheart rejoining the show, and bringing her new partner who fans think looks like Ronnie Ortiz-Magro with her wasn’t enough, there’s a long-lost family reunion going on in season 7.

Thursday’s episode (February 15) sees the Jersey Shore: Family Vacation star meet her biological father for the first time.

The MTV star also met her half-sister, and her father’s partner during the meeting.

Angelina says ‘nothing compares’

During Jersey Shore: Family Reunion, Angelina explains how “the man she thought was her father” didn’t walk her down the aisle.

So, when her biological dad suggested walking her down the aisle, she became emotional and said she was “going to cry.”

She said all she really wanted in her life was to “have a father” adding: “Nothing in this world could compare to finally meeting my biological dad and hugging this man.”

When the two first met, Angelina said she could “feel” her father “shaking,” adding how he must’ve been “very nervous.”

He said it’s “not really nerve-wracking, it’s disturbing that I didn’t know, I could’ve watched you grown and stuff like that, be a part of your life…”

Fans see a likeness

When Angelina and her father first meet, her half-sister and his partner agree the two look alike.

Angelina also agreed and said: “Same big eyes.”

Her fans and followers also thought the same, and commented on the Jersey Shore Family Vacation clip, writing: “She looks like her dad so much.”

Another said: “I mean how insane how alike they already are!!!”

Not only did the two find similarities in their looks, but they also agreed their personalities weren’t all that different, either.
