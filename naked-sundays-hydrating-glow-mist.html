The Hydrating Glow Mist from Australian (and vegan!) brand Naked Sundays makes reapplying sunscreen such a breeze. It’s weightless and adds the most beautiful glow to your complexion. What it doesn’t do is leave behind an oily, greasy, sticky film that messes up your existing makeup look. The transparent mist’s hydrating properties can be attributed to hyaluronic acid, which locks in moisture. Add even more vitamin C to the mix with Kakadu plum, an ingredient that’s usually found in Northern Australia and also contains vitamin E, folic acid, and carotenoids. All of these elements help hydrate and illuminate skin.

You might be wondering when to spritz the Hydrating Glow Mist onto your face if it’s both a skincare and SPF product. According to Naked Sundays, here’s how to use it: 

  • Close eyes and purse lips
  • Spray in a circular motion 8 inches away from your face
  • Spray at least 7 times
  • Ensure you use your SPF spray as a top-up only
  • Be sure to top up your SPF every 90 minutes when outdoors
  • Can be used on face and body

The emphasis here is that the Hydrating Glow Mist is a sunscreen topper, which means you’re still going to want to apply your usual chemical or mineral SPF in the morning after your skincare routine and before your makeup regimen. This Naked Sundays product is intended for sunscreen touch-ups (a.k.a. reapplication). Mist the product over bare skin or on top of makeup, but definitely be sure to pack a bottle in your bag so you can reapply as needed.

Pro tip: the SPF50+ Hydrating Glow Mist can even help top up your décolletage and limbs too.
