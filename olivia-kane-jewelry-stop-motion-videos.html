This Jewelry Brand's Stop-Motion Videos Will Be Your Newest Obsession

Publish date: 2024-05-18

Olivia Kane jewelry can catch your eye just as well as it can capture your mood. The brand, which specializes in fine-jewelry mood rings, just released a set of collage videos for its newest campaign, done in collaboration with New York-based artist Zuzu Snyder. The result is three artistic stop-motion clips in whimsical prints and hues that are almost as beautiful as the jewelry.

Courtesy Olivia Kane

BAZAAR.com's senior features editor Olivia Fleming, whose middle name is Kane, founded the jewelry line just last year with the unique intent of creating sophisticated mood rings for adults. (It also didn't hurt that she had a long-time obsession with the 1991 film My Girl, especially Vada's mood ring scene.) The pieces are chic and delicate, handmade in Brooklyn with semi-precious stones and recycled 14k rose gold, yellow gold and silver.

Watch all three videos below and shop the pieces at oliviakane.co.
