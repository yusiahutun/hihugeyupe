Best Parasol Christmas Tree 2023: Pet-Friendly Christmas Tree

Publish date: 2024-04-08

Any pet owner knows the pain of expertly decorating the Christmas tree with delicate baubles only for them to be sent flying by an overly enthusiastic pet. However, Habitat's dog-friendly parasol Christmas tree might mean you can hang onto your treasured trinkets for a bit longer.

Reaching the same height as a normal tree, the 6ft design has an extra-long stem that keeps the bottom branches safe from cheery dogs (or even small children) and should dissuade cats from attempting to reach the top.

Habitat 6ft Half Parasol Christmas Tree

Habitat 6ft Half Parasol Christmas Tree

As well as the green spruce above, Habitat also has this charming white version, complete with a generous smattering of faux snow. It's a great pick if you'd prefer the winter wonderland look, and it's versatile enough to be dressed up with both neutral and colourful baubles.

Habitat 6ft Snowy Half Design Christmas Tree

Habitat 6ft Snowy Half Design Christmas Tree

These trees are certainly something different and won't be to everyone's taste. But if you find yourself worrying to the point you can't properly enjoy the season's festivities, one of these half trees might be worth considering for your peace of mind.

We can also see them working well for smaller spaces (and there's certainly plenty of room for presents underneath).

Ultimately, if switching to one of these trees helps us to hang onto our decorations rather than purchasing new ones, we think it’s well worth it. You can buy Habitat's parasol pet-friendly Christmas tree here.
