How to Play Games With Your Friends on Telegram

Publish date: 2024-03-15

Telegram has long evolved beyond a simple means of communication. It's become a versatile platform that offers many features, and one of the fun ones is the ability to play games right within the messenger.

The Gamee bot enables this feature and allows you to have a blast with your friends while staying connected. All without breaking a sweat.

How to Play Games on Telegram With Gamee

Bots are one of the main sources of Telegram's cool features. Among the impressive game bots available on Telegram, Gamee stands out as it provides access to dozens of games in a single location. This verified bot has a diverse selection of games, ranging from brain-teasing puzzles to exciting arcade-style challenges.

Close

To start playing games on your own, all you have to do is message the Gamee bot. You can do this by searching for Gamee in the Telegram search bar or using Gamee bot's Telegram link. Once you're in Gamee's chat, tap Start. Gamee will then provide you with a list of popular games. Tap a game and tap Play solo under the message to start playing.

The true fun in these games lies in sharing the joy with friends. Gamee is equipped to share a specific game with a group chat and keep track of everyone's scores.

Close

Using an inline command, you can summon Gamee to any private or group chat. Just type @gamee in your message, and Gamee will display a list of all available games. Pick a game, send it to your friends, and let the fun begin!

If you already have a particular game in mind, you can search for it by typing its name after @gamee. Your friends can do this too, and you can all play the same game together.

Chatting and Playing, All in One

Playing games with friends on Telegram is a fun way to stay connected and engage in friendly competition. Now that you know just how easy it is, try out the Gamee bot on Telegram, and you might just discover a new background activity to enjoy when chatting with your friends.

Games aren't the only cool thing about Telegram. If you enjoyed this feature, be sure to explore all the other awesome features Telegram has to offer to get the most out of the app.

FAQ

Q: Is Telegram Good for Gaming?

Telegram is an instant messaging app, but it offers several games for you to play solo as well as with your friends. If you're okay with classic games and don't fancy high-graphics games (like the ones you play on your computers), Telegram is an okay place to enjoy some gaming time with your friends.

Q: How Many Games Are on Telegram?

According to an unofficial source, Telegram has about 30 games that you can play solo as well as with your friends. The majority of these games are offered by @gamee.

Q: How to Play Multiplayer in Telegram?

You can play games with your friends on Telegram by using a group chat and a game bot like @gamee. This setup allows you to share your games with your friends and have a fun time together.
