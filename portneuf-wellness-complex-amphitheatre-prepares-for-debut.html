Portneuf Wellness Complex Amphitheatre Prepares For Debut

Publish date: 2024-05-23

POCATELLO, ID Workers are putting the finishing touches on the Portneuf Wellness Complex's amphitheatre just days ahead of its first concert on August 31st.

The Beach Boys and the Nitty Gritty Dirt Band will be the first performers to take the stage at the 11,000-capacity shed.

"A lot of people are still up (at the amphitheatre) running around trying to finish up the little odds and ends," structural architect Keevan Shropshire told local ABC affiliate KIDK. "It's looking very good and I'm very positive things are going to run smoothly." – Staff Writers
