RAGBRAI riders show college pride

Publish date: 2024-10-04

by: Staff

Posted: Jul 28, 2017 / 07:10 PM CDT

Updated: Jul 28, 2017 / 07:10 PM CDT

Riders are wearing their favorite teams’ jerseys as they ride from Cresco to Waukon today on RAGBRAI. Day six of the ride is jersey day and lots of passionate fans are on the road. Panther fans, Joe and Lori Snyder hope their jersey’s will help them get through the tough hills on day six. They say their favorite part of the trip–all the different people on the road.

“I love to see the unicyclists and everything else out there. It’s really great to see all the different people out there.”

Today’s trek is a 60.1 mile ride from Cresco to Waukon with stops in towns like, Decorah, Ossian and Castalia.
