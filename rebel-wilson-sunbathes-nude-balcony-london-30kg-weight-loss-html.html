Rebel Wilson sunbathes nude on a balcony in London after 30kg weight loss

Publish date: 2024-03-23

She's gone through quite the transformation, after losing a jaw-dropping 30 kilograms (66lbs or 4.7st) last year.

And Rebel Wilson showcased her newfound confidence on Monday as she stripped down for a nude sunbathing session.

The Pitch Perfect star, 41, shared a raunchy video on Instagram of herself soaking up the sun naked while lying on her balcony in London.

Sunseeker: Rebel Wilson stripped down for a nude sunbathing session in London on Monday

She gazed into the lens before winking and offering a beaming smile to fans as the brief clip came to an end.

'When the sun comes out in London I immediately leap to naked sunbaking,' the screen star captioned the video.

The star announced in November she had reached her goal weight of 75kg (165lbs or 11.7st), and topped off her Year of Health by appearing on Ant's adventure show.

Sydney native Rebel lost 30 kilograms (66.1lbs or 4.7st) as she overhauled her lifestyle in an incredibly impressive feat.

Basking in the sunshine: She gazed into the lens before winking and offering a beaming smile to fans as the brief clip came to an end 

Since then, she has showed off her body transformation by posting photos of her trim physique in workout gear and stylish ensembles.

Rebel had documented her 2020 lifestyle overhaul on social media, including her fitness, diet, mindset and motivational secrets.

According to People magazine, Rebel's remarkable transformation is partially down to following the Mayr Method diet plan.

'It's an approach that eliminates food intolerances, reduces sugar, encourages eating whole foods slowly, boosts the immune system and reduces inflammation,' a source said.

'She exercises with a personal trainer up to six times a week, goes on walks and is trying to up her protein intake nutritionally.'

Working hard: The Hollywood star made 2020 her 'year of health' and worked hard to overhaul her diet and fitness habits, losing an impressive 30kg. Pictured right in 2014

Last month, the actress spoke about being treated differently since her dramatic weight loss.

She said on The Morning Crew with Hughesy, Ed and Erin that it was 'interesting' to see people's attitudes change following her transformation.  

'I think what's been really interesting is how other people treat you. Sometimes being bigger, people didn't necessarily look twice at you,' Rebel explained.

'Now that I'm in good shape, people offer to carry my groceries to the car and hold doors open for you,' she added. 'I'm like, is this what other people experienced all the time?'

She added: 'I also find it interesting that people pay so much attention to a weight loss transformation, when there's so much going on in the world.'

Well documented: Rebel had documented her 2020 lifestyle overhaul on social media, including her fitness, diet, mindset and motivational secrets 

Rebel said last month that she's in a great place following her split from boyfriend Jacob Busch.

The star first revealed she was dating the 29-year-old millionaire in September, posting a series of loved-up photos with her new beau on Instagram. 

According to People, they were introduced by a mutual friend in 2019, but didn't start dating until Rebel returned to the USA after spending time in Australia last year.

'They are officially boyfriend and girlfriend. He's a gentleman, has very old school manners and treats her well,' a source said at the time. 

Rebel and Jacob enjoyed a series of romantic holidays during their whirlwind relationship, travelling to far-flung places such as Mexico and Monaco. 

People: 'I think what's been really interesting is how other people treat you. Sometimes being bigger, people didn't necessarily look twice at you,' she said. Pictured left in December 2019 

She hinted at their break-up on Instagram on January 16, writing: 'Been a rough week for so many reasons... but I'm so grateful to have such loving friends and family.' 

They were last pictured together on December 24 when they embarked on a pre-Christmas ski trip with friends in Colorado. 

A source told Page Six last month the relationship had 'just run its course'.

In an interview with People in November, Rebel said she was determined to find love, and 'deliberately went out and dated a lot of different people' before Jacob.   

'I wasn't very experienced in love and relationships... and so I went out and just gave it my all, tried really hard to find love and stuff,' she added. 

Ex factor: Last month, it was revealed that Rebel had split from her boyfriend of less than a year, Anheuser-Busch brewery heir Jacob Busch (left)

Speaking at the Television Critics Association panel in Los Angeles last month, the actress confirmed she was 'still single' and dating.

Rebel also said she'd been 'shamelessly' showing off her weight loss on Instagram and was 'feeling great' after shedding the kilos.

The comedian joked she was enlisting the help of her stylist on the TV show Pooch Perfect because her weight loss hadn't improved her dating prospects.

'I got two looks per episode. I got to work with my stylist and show off the new physique because I'm still single!' she said. 'This is my prime-time opportunity to just really put it out there.'

Rebel then revealed, perhaps jokingly, that she'd already been 'asked out by two contestants' on Pooch Perfect, a reality show about competitive dog grooming.

'The twist is that the contestants were related and they were actually mother and son,' she added.

Isn't It Romantic star Rebel is in London for work, but also made time to catch up with her longtime friend Matt Lucas on Saturday.

The British comedian played Rebel's roommate in the 2011 comedy film Bridesmaids, and the pair were also once roommates.

They shared photos on Instagram of themselves wandering around the English capital and checking out street art.

Rebel captioned the snaps, 'Only gays in the village' - a cheeky reference to Matt's character Daffyd Thomas in Little Britain.
