Best clothing alteration and restoration services

Publish date: 2024-05-30

We earn a commission for products purchased through some links in this article.

The Seam

MORE

The Seam is an online restoration service, which is exclusively partnering with Net-a-Porter, Mr Porter and The Outnet as the retailers' first dedicated repair service. It is aiming to help extend the life of its luxury products for its customers, who can choose from clothing alterations and repairs, bespoke customisations, care and repair for handbags, footwear and jewellery services.

Sojo

MORE

Alterations app Sojo connects its users to local seamsters and tailoring businesses, then delivers your items to be altered or repaired via bicycle (the greenest option). Its mission is simple: to help people make their own wardrobes more circular and sustainable by ensuring that the alteration and repair process is as easy and fuss-free as possible.

Selfridges Repair

MORE

Selfridges offers its customers a repair service from the comfort of their own homes. Book a virtual consultation with one of the London department store's experts and choose from an in-store appointment or an at-home collection. The repair concierge experts will work hard to make your loved item as good as new. Take your shoes, accessories, jewellery, eyewear or get a replacement battery for your favourite watch. You're in safe hands here.

The Restory

MORE

The Restory provides aftercare for luxury fashion you can trust, giving your most treasured pieces a little TLC. The company is dedicated to looking after your shoes, bags and leather goods by cutting out the middle man and bringing the service conveniently to your doorstep. Your items will be picked up, worked on and delivered sparkling new.

The Handbag Clinic

MORE

The Handbag Clinic is focused on designer handbags, offering a buy, sell and restore service, which aims to prolong the life of your beloved accessories.

Harrods x The Restory

MORE

Harrods – one of London's most iconic department stores – works with The Restory to help its valued shoppers give their favourite accessories a new lease of life with trusted aftercare. Whether you're in need of stain removal or a colour renewal, book in a collection and Harrods will take care of the rest with its range of services for shoes, bags and other leather goods.

Reture

MORE

Reture takes restoration to the next level. The service extends your garment's life by upcycling and altering your unworn pieces into something new. Users are connected directly to designers via Reture; choose a creative, send your item, work on the design together and then receive your brand new piece to wear without having to buy anything new.

Harvey Nichols x The Restory

MORE

Similar to Harrods, Harvey Nichols also works with The Restory to provide an on-demand aftercare service to its shoppers. It works in the same way by making the restoring process as accessible as possible: send your piece, approve your quote, and then receive it feeling brand new.
