Rob Dyrdek Lists His MTV Reality Show Pad!

Publish date: 2024-08-20

Rob Dyrdek is on a real-estate roll!

Just last month the 38-year-old dropped $2.5 million on a newly remodeled 4 bedroom, 3.5 bathroom Hollywood Hills abode.

Now, the professional skateboarder has listed another pricey pad: His former reality show stomping grounds.

Dyrdek bought the house back in 2005 for $1.4 mil – and the property was regularly featured on his hilarious series "Rob & Big."

The 3 bedroom, 3.5 bathroom, 2,792 square-foot home boasts hardwood floors, a spacious kitchen, stainless steel appliances, vaulted ceilings, and sliding glass doors throughout which give the place plenty of sun.

There's even an additional lower level which comes equipped with a personal theater and a separate den -- perfect for hosting Hollywood parties!

Really, can you imagine the crazy stunts Rob's pulled here?

The best part of his former bachelor pad: The spectacular views! The property sits atop the Hollywood Hill, and with two giant balconies you can see both the the Valley and the city.

Want to live like a professional skateboarder? Rob's playground can all be yours for cool $1.249 million!

Contact Laura Kellam, Prudential CA - Beverly Hills, she hold the listing.
