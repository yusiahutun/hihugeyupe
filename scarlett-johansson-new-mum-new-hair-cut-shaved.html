New mum Scarlett Johansson rocks undercut

Publish date: 2024-07-28

    Scarlett Johansson looked incredible as she hit the red carpet sporting a dramatic new hair 'do at the weekend. The new mum rocked an undercut as she attended a gala in Australia. It's a difficult hairstyle to pull off, but with her feline features and chiselled cheekbones, the partial buzzcut more than suits the stunning actress.

    The actress more than pulls off the dramatic new haircut

    The actress welcomed her first child back in September with husband Roman Dauriac. But she seems to have pinged right back into shape, flaunting her slim physique in a tight-fitting halter-neck dress from Preen's Spring 2015 collection. The quirky black dress featured red and white trim and fringing. Scarlett accessorised with a pair of classic black heels.

    Scarlett was showing off her enviable post-baby physique in a tight-fitting dress

    She kept her jewellery to a minimum, sporting a simple black bracelet and pearl earrings. A slick of peach lipstick completed the look. Scarlett walked the red carpet with Chris Hemsworth, her co-star. She presented the excellence in film award to Chris during the ceremony at the 2015 G'Day USA Gala and Australian Academy of Cinema and Television Arts International Awards.

    The actress was presenting an award to her co-star Chris Hemsworth

    "He's all at once sensitive and ferocious, otherworldly and grounded, is shockingly handsome and is surprisingly approachable," Scarlett said of her co-star before handing him the award. "He is every man and like no man you've met before. He is versatile despite his physicality, which makes him a grade A movie star." Scarlett recently opened up about her new role of motherhood. "It's wonderful. It's very, very magical – and exhausting," she told People. "The love is just unbelievable. It's very overwhelming," she added of her connection with her baby . While the blonde beauty has previously admitted that she is still learning the ropes, Scarlett has thrown herself into her new role and seems more than happy to be on diaper duty and to do the late-night feeds. "[I do] the whole bit," she said. "I'm nursing and I love it."
