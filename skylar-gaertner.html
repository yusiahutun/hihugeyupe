Career:

Skylar Gaertner started his career at a very small age, when he was 4 years. He believes that his talent of acting is god gifted. The name of his debut project was “Lipstick Jungle” in the year 2008.

Some of his popular movies, shows, and TV series are Nurse Jackie, CSI, Dare Devil, Request and many more. The series which got him limelight was “Ozark” in the year 2017.

He had also been nominated in the year 2021 and in 2019 in the category of Outstanding Performance by an Ensemble in a Drama Series for his film “Ozark”.

Skylar, who plays Jonah Byrde on Netflix crime thriller, auditioned for the lead role in the fantasy dram we series named “Stranger Things” but he didn’t get the role. Later he said that he is glad that he didn’t get the part and he added: ‘Yeah, I guess. I wouldn’t have been on Ozark and Ozark’s pretty great so.’
