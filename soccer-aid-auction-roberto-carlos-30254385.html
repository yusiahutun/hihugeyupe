Unexpected star's Soccer Aid shirt going for over three times more than Roberto Carlos'

Publish date: 2024-10-02

Soccer Aid 2023 saw some greats of world football turnout at Old Trafford for a good cause.

Jill Scott, Jermain Defoe, Karen Carney, Gary Cahill, Jack Wilshere, Gary Neville, Paul Scholes, Eni Aluko, David James and Nicky Butt turned out for England.

Meanwhile, the World XI boasted the likes of Heather O’Reilly, Kalyn Kyle, Ben Foster, Nani, Francesco Totti, Gabriel Batistuta, Hernan Crespo, Patrice Evra, Robbie Keane and Roberto Carlos.

It would be fair to suggest the last name on that list was one of the greatest full-backs to have ever laced up a pair of football boots.

During his lengthy career, Roberto Carlos lifted the World Cup along with multiple Champions League and La Liga titles whilst plying his trade for Real Madrid.

So when it was announced that shirts from Soccer Aid, which saw the World XI win 4-2 against England, were to be auctioned off for charity, you would expect Carlos' strip to be worth more than his fellow pros and celebrity team-mates.

However, one Hollywood A-lister who appeared at Old Trafford playing for England has had his shirt auctioned off for over three times that of Carlos'.

As of now, Marvel star Tom Hiddleston's shirt is currently going for over £1,800, while Carlos' match worn signed shirt is going for just £504.

The shirts are being auctioned off by Match Worn Shirt, with all proceeds being raised going to UNICEF.

So popular is Hiddlestone's jersey, it is currently the most valuable shirt of the entire list of Soccer Aid stars who turned out at Old Trafford.

And the shirts aren't the only thing being auctioned off in the name of a good cause. signed boots worn by the likes of Lionel Messi, Luis Suarez, Neymar, Crespo, Scholes, Totti and Pochettino also up for grabs.

Follow the Soccer Aid shirt auctions and bid yourself at https://www.matchwornshirt.com/category/soccer-aid.
