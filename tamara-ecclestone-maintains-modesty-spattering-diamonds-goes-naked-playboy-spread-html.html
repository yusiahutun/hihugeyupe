She had a show called Billion $$ Girl, and is one of the richest young heiresses in the UK.

So when it came to Tamara Ecclestone posing naked for Playboy magazine, the 28-year-old opted for spattering of diamonds to maintain her modesty.

The pretty brunette socialite is seen with sparking crystals on her breasts, holding a larger crystal on her lower body to ensure she didn't display too much.

Diamond diva: Tamara Ecclestone poses nude with just some crystals preserving her modesty as she covers Playboy magazine

Wearing just a pair of sky-high metallic heels, Tamara lays on top of a crystal Playboy bunny, with her brunette hair fanned out around her.

Tamara covers the May issue of Playboy magazine, out on Friday, as well as featuring in an eight-page pictorial spread inside.

Another image from the magazine shows Tamara wearing a towel wrapped on top of her head, with a pair of Playboy sunglasses as she smokes a cigarette from a retro holder.

Shady lady: Another shot shows Tamara wearing just a towel on her head with a pair of sunglasses as she smokes a cigarette in a retro holder

Cheeky: Tamara shared another picture from the shoot earlier this month

A further image, which the heiress tweeted earlier this month, shows a pouting Tamara sprawled against a wall, her dress unzipped and bottom exposed.

Speaking about the new shoot, Tamara said: 'I am so proud of this beautiful shoot. As a woman, I believe you have to embrace your body, and feel beautiful both inside and out.

'Playboy is so iconic, and for nearly 60 years, the magazine has featured some of the world’s most beautiful women in its pages.

'I am extremely honoured to be part of this legacy as the magazine’s May cover girl.'

Dressed down: Tamara wore a pair of optical illusion trousers and went make-up free as she headed out for dinner in London over the weekend

In the accompanying interview, Tamara says that while she knows she will never be able to replicate the success of her Formula 1 mogul father Bernie, she still wants to make a name for herself as a businesswoman in her own right.

She explained: 'Every time I come here, people say to me, "Oh you're the European Paris Hilton". I'm the opposite of that.

'I know I'm never going to be as successful as my dad, but I get bored doing nothing. I couldn't go from vacation to vacation and have no motivation.'

'I won't be as successful as my dad': Tamara said she knows she won't ever be able to fully replicate the success her father Bernie has achieved in his life

And while Bernie might be less than impressed with Tamara's new shoot, one person who can't comment is her mother Slavica - who posed for her fair share of shoots back in the day.

Asked if her mother had warned her against stripping off for Playboy, Tamara said: 'She'd be a bit of a hypocrite if she told me not to.'

Tamara added: 'I don't have a problem with nakedness.'

Tamara's Playboy issue is on newsstands and on i.Playboy.com Friday, April 19, with photos also available online at Playboy.com/tamara.
