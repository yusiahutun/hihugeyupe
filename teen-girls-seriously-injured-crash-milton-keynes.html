Three teen girls seriously injured after being hit by Mercedes in Milton Keynes

Publish date: 2024-06-08

THREE teen girls have been seriously injured after being hit by a car.

The girls were walking near Linford Wood in Milton Keynes when they were struck by a black Mercedes E Class.

Two of the teens were rushed to Milton Keynes University hospital while the third was taken to the John Radcliffe hospital in Oxford.

All three suffered serious injuries in the horror just before 5.30pm on Saturday.

The driver stopped at the scene and did not require hospital treatment.

Police have now launched an investigation and are appealing for witnesses.

PC Christopher Ruvolo-Jones of the Joint Operations Roads Policing Unit, based at Milton Keynes, said; “We are appealing for witnesses following this collision.

“We would like to talk to anyone who may have witnessed this collision or seen the pedestrian’s prior to entering the H3 Monk’s Way dual carriageway.

“Additionally if anyone was travelling on the dual carriageway around the time of the incident and has a dash-cam we would ask them to check their footage and get in touch if it shows anything which maybe of assistance to our investigation."

Anyone with information can call Thames Valley Police on 101 quoting reference 1555 16/04/2022.
