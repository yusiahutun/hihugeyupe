The Bachelor winners 'leaked' as one of the leading men could go home ALONE

Publish date: 2024-02-17

The finale of The Bachelors Australia is set to air late next month - but fans already think they have picked the winners.

The odds suggest Bachelors Ben and Luke will find romance, while Wesley may end season 11 by going home alone.

It comes after The Bachelors Australia premiered to a mere 224,000 metro viewers on Sunday night, its lowest premiere audience in franchise history.
