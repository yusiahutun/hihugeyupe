Impact on mental health

While masturbation has good effects on our mental and physical well-being. But on the other side, it also has some impacts on mental health.

Feeling Of Guilt/Shame:

Masturbation can lead to feelings of guilt and shame in people due to many reasons. In many sections of society, masturbation is still a taboo, something that makes people impure or a wrongdoing. There is a stigma attached to self-pleasure. As self-pleasure is termed as dirty in many people’s eyes. This guilt and shame also come due to a number of spiritual, and cultural factors, as masturbation is considered a sinful act. The chances of developing anxiety, self-worth-related issues, and stress also increase.

In many cases, people also refuse to talk about it as they feel that this can affect their overall image in society. As a result, this avoidance of sex education topic can severely affect teenagers who are going toward their adulthood.

Addiction in Some Cases:

Masturbation is a perfectly normal, beneficiary process, but when it becomes an addiction, this can develop several psychological conditions. When this activity takes place in a healthy way, then it has a number of pros for our body, but when it becomes compulsive it can interfere with your daily life activities. The addiction to masturbation has a severe impact on your relationships as it can take you away from your partner or start neglecting their feelings. Therefore, it is important to address the issue and ask for help as it will allow you to get rid of the addiction of this habit and provide a healthier life.
