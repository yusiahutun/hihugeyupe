Travis Kelce responds to 'brads and chads' booing Taylor - 'absolutely insane'

Publish date: 2024-02-12

Trust Travis Kelce to stand up for Taylor Swift. The 34-year-old NFL stud recently discussed Taylor Swift’s impact when she’s in the stadium with his older brother, Jason Kelce.

Taylor Swift is taking a break from her record-breaking Eras tour, but she’s not cooped up at home. She has been spotted at multiple Kansas City Chiefs matches supporting new beau Travis Kelce. As always, when the music star is spotted at the VIP gallery, cameras can’t help but cut to her and even the opposition’s fans cheer for the Miss Americana and Heartbreak Princess.

Travis Kelce is not bothered by ‘Brads and Chads’ booing at Taylor Swift

Taylor’s boyfriend Travis Kelce finally responded to some fans booing the singer at a recent match, but he is not bothered by the ‘Brads and Chads.’ Instead, he swoons over the music icon’s incredible star power that even gets the rival team in good spirits.

The 34-year-old Catching Kelce star shares his reaction in his latest podcast New Heights.

He told his brother, who also co-hosts the pod: “I’ll say this: They showed Taylor at the game. You don’t see an entire home team fan base go insane for somebody wearing the opposite team’s colors. Just shows you how amazing that girl is. They went absolutely insane when they showed Taylor on the screen, yes.”

The celebrity mentions the booing incident as well, saying: “There might have been a few Brads and Chads that were booing. But for the most part, everyone was f****** screaming their tail off for her.”

Travis Kelce struggles to ‘play it cool’

The Kansas City Chiefs tight end is clearly in love and is rumored to be settling down with the singer.

The NFL star recalls how the stadium went ‘insane’ for T. Swizzle but he had to keep his emotions in check as he was still playing.

He said: “I was trying to keep it cool: I was like, ‘Don’t show your cards, don’t show your cards.’ It was fun, man.” The star player also gave a shout-out to his rival team for hosting a fun game and treating his girlfriend right.

“So shoutout to the Patriots, New England,” he added. “She’s on record saying that the stadium is one of the funnest that she’s played at so for them to show her is just them showing her some love, so shoutout to the Patriots for doing that.“
