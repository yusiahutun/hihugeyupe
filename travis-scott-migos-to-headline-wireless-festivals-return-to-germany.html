Travis Scott, Migos To Headline Wireless Festivals Return To Germany

Publish date: 2024-04-29

FRANKFURT, Germany (CelebrityAccess) — After a brief pause in 2018, Live Nation announced that the Wireless Festival would return to Germany next year, with headliners Travis Scott and Migos leading the bill.

Wireless 2019 is scheduled to take place on July 5th and 6th at a new location – the 40,000-capacity Alter Rebstockpark in Frankfurt. For its debut in 2017, the Wireless Festival was held at Frankfurt’s Commerzbank Arena.

Billed as an Urban Music & Lifestyle festival, Wireless Germany announced a lineup that includes Hayley Kiyoko, James Blake, J. Balvin, Rae Sremmurd, Rita Ora, KC Rebel, UFO361, Shirin David, and Azet & Zuna, as well as homegrown Germany hip-hop artists Marteria & Casper and Bonez MC & RAF Camora.

The festival will also feature breakdance performances, street art, fashion & styling, chillout areas to open-air hookah bars, as well as a selection of street food stalls, cocktail bars and food trucks

Two-day GA tickets are priced from €139, which also includes tickets for public transport to and from Rebstock Park and camping options are not available.
