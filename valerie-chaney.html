Professional Career

After completing her graduation, Valerie Chaney worked as a middle school teacher in Northern California. During that time as a teacher, she discovered how young girls struggled with having low self-esteem, and this played a major influence in her advocacy of helping women with the same problem to overcome it.

She became involved with the REALgirl Empowerment Program as a result of her not-so-perfect childhood and what she saw happening among her kids. It helps young girls between the ages of nine and sixteen with problems with self-esteem while also educating them about their potential so they can fully explore their potential as individuals.

She works with young girls in the Los Angeles region as a REALgirl® Instructor, helping them overcome issues like body shaming and identity doubt while also instilling confidence in them so they can stand on their own. She believes that this would inspire more women to stand up and become future social leaders.

Moreover, she began teaching “Wild and Free” dance and meditation lessons in 2019 at Studio A Dance in the Hyperion Arts Complex in Los Angeles. She rose to fame after marrying Pete Holmes who is a well-known comedian, actor, writer, and producer.

Pete Holmes

Pete Holmes is a well-known comedian, actor, writer, and podcast host. He began his career in comedy in the early 2000s, performing stand-up comedy in various clubs and venues across the country. He gained recognition for his energetic and animated style, as well as his witty and often self-deprecating humor.

Moreover, he appeared on numerous late-night talk shows, including “The Tonight Show with Conan O’Brien” and “Late Night with Jimmy Fallon,” further establishing his presence in the comedy scene.

He has written for several television shows. Additionally, he was a writer for the animated series “Ugly Americans” and “Outsourced.” He also wrote for the comedy sketch show “In the Heights” and was a voice actor for the animated series “American Dad!”

In 2013, he began hosting his late-night talk show, “The Pete Holmes Show,” which aired on TBS. The show featured interviews with celebrity guests, comedy sketches, and stand-up performances.

Although the show only ran for two seasons, it garnered a loyal fan base. Following the cancellation of his talk show, he launched a podcast called “You Made It Weird with Pete Holmes.” The podcast features long-form conversations with various guests, including comedians, actors, and musicians.

Recent Years

He appeared as a lead in the 2022 sitcom “How We Roll” playing the role of Tom Smallwood. Meanwhile, he appeared as Rand (Abby’s Fiancé) in the 2023 sitcom “Night Court” appearing in the episode ‘Marathon-Thon-Thon-Thon-Thon.’

Moreover, he has also appeared in films such as “Don’t Think Twice,” “The Secret Life of Pets 2,” and “Home Sweet Home Alone,” among others.
