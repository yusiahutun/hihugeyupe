After watching one episode of Netflix’s Beckham documentary, you’ll be left wanting to (Posh) spice up your wardrobe. Move over Hailey Bieber, Bella Hadid and Kendall Jenner - Victoria Beckham was the original street style queen.

The star has had quite the transition through the decades, evolving from musical icon to style icon, WAG to business mogul and Spice Girl to IT girl with her Victoria Beckham collection. 

We can all agree she has long been a muse of ours, and we have noticed that even Victoria takes inspiration from her past outfits. VB is proof that everything comes back around in the world of fashion - even Nicola Peltz channelled her Y2K style in red leather at the documentary premiere...bravo!

Inspired? Browse our roundup of Posh picks that are major throwbacks...

The series offers a large dose of nineties nostalgia, which showcases plenty of Y2K trends the fashionista nailed...think bodycon dresses, oversized bags and flared jeans, which just so happen to be the craze today too. 

Victoria has always had a flare (pun intended) for denim - remember her WAG era? Way before creating the Victoria Beckham empire that took the catwalk by storm, she started with a denim collection called DVB, which included bedazzled pockets, low rises and flared legs.

This has clearly had an influence on her today - minus the bling - as Victoria wore a pair on the documentary. We can confirm: the Beckhams do have good jeans, after all!
