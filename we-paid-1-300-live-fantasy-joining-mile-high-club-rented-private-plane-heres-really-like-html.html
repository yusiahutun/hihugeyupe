We paid $1,300 to live out our fantasy of joining the mile-high club in a rented private plane - her

Publish date: 2024-08-04

A couple has candidly shared what it is like on board a plane offering the 'mile high club' experience from $1,300.

Caitlyn Rose, originally from Australia, took to TikTok to give a glimpse inside the Love Cloud based in Las Vegas.

The twin engine Cessna 414 aircraft offers couples the chance to live out their fantasy in the 'custom made romantic interior.'

But other social media users seemed less convinced and rushed to the comments to share their disbelief.

It then cuts to show the social media star striking a sultry pose in a skimpy black dress from on board.

The cabin has been lavishly decked out with 'sex position pillows and cushions,' red satin sheets and a foam mattress.

'We captured out memories. It's the perfect date night.'

The blonde beauty, who has more than 490,000 followers on the video sharing platform, later added a follow-up video that stated: 'Don't worry, the pilots cannot hear you. 

'Sit back and enjoy or record your memories for fun like we did.'

The Love Cloud's official website itself states: 'You and the pilot are separated by a secured curtain door. 

'The pilot is also equipped with a noise canceling headset so that you and your partner have complete privacy throughout the flight.'

Pricing varies depending on the length of flight starting at $1,295 per couple for 45 minutes up to $1,895 for 90 minutes.

Upon landing, couples are presented with a Mile High Club VIP Certificate as well as having a photo taken with the aircraft itself. 

But other social media users seemed less convinced as one wrote: 'The amount of people who did it in that exact spot... nah I'm good.'

Another added: 'Imagine being that pilot.'
