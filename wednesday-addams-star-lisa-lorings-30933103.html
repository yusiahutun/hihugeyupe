Wednesday Addams star Lisa Loring's tragic life porn star husband to gruesome find

Publish date: 2024-09-15

The Addams Family star Lisa Loring had a tragic life up until her untimely death aged just 64.

Best known for playing the original Wednesday Addams in the sitcom from 1964 until 1966, Lisa died in January 2023 at Providence St Joseph Medical Centre in Burbank, California.

She had suffered a “massive stroke” caused by smoking and high blood pressure, and died from complications after three days on life support, her daughter confirmed.

But throughout her life, Lisa had to battle numerous tragedies, from the loss of her mother to numerous divorces.

Just five years old when she was cast as Wednesday, Lisa started her career even earlier, and at just three she was working as a child model.

Lisa’s parents divorced when she was still a child, and her mother Judith was battling alcoholism throughout her daughter’s younger years.

In 1974, Judith died aged just 34, leaving a 16-year-old Lisa motherless.

Turbulent love life

Lisa got married for the first time when she was only 15, to her childhood sweetheart Farrell Foumberg. The pair welcomed a daughter, Vanessa, together, but their marriage ended just one year after they tied the knot.

In 1981, Lisa married for the second time, this time to actor Doug Stevenson – but their relationship came to an end after two years and welcoming a second daughter, Marianne.

In 2003 Lisa married for the last time to Graham Rich, remaining with him until 2014.

But it was her third husband who caused the most controversy, as Lisa settled down with adult film star Jerry Butler.

Porn star hubby

Lisa met her porn star husband Jerry on the set of 1987 adult film Traci’s Big Trick, where she was working as a make-up artist and an uncredited writer.

Despite meeting in such a racy environment, Lisa wasn’t happy with Jerry’s continued involvement in porn and he was forced to fly under the radar to continue shooting films without his wife finding out.

Jerry himself admitted he was “addicted” to the porn lifestyle during an interview with Dateline NBC, and the couple discussed how his career was damaging their marriage on the Sally Jessy Raphael Show.

Lisa admitted: “We had an agreement that he would not, absolutely not, continue doing adult films,” before she discovered he was going behind her back.

But despite efforts to mend things, the pair divorced in 1992 and Jerry retired from porn soon after.

Jerry died from cancer aged 58 back in 2018.

Gruesome discovery

During her marriage, Lisa turned to illegal drugs to help her cope with Jerry’s lies, and soon became addicted to heroin.

Things only got worse when, in 1991, she discovered the body of Kelly Van Dyke – her husband’s daughter and niece to Dick Van Dyke – after she hanged herself.

Kelly, who was 33, was found dead in her bedroom after calling her husband speaking about killing herself.

Lisa was so disturbed by what she had seen that she tried to overdose, but failed, later checking herself into rehab in an attempt to ditch her substance abuse issues.

“I was disillusioned about my adult life,” she confessed at the time. She also quit acting and switched to a regular day job at an interior design company.

Lisa became “afraid of rejection” when it came to getting back into acting, instead choosing to appear at fan conventions selling autographs until her death earlier this year.
