Whiskers for Whiskey Returns Next Week

Publish date: 2024-09-28

The Humane Society of the Treasure Coast will partner with The Chef’s Table and Libations Mobile Catering Co. for Whiskers for Whiskey, a four-course whiskey tasting and pairing event on September 27 at 6 p.m. at 2313 SE Ocean Boulevard.

Representatives from High West Distillery in Park City, Utah, will be on site to guide attendees through a flavorful tour intended to unlock the senses. Chef-prepared cuisine will accompany each pour.

Tickets are $150 per person and are available at hstc1.org. Each guest will be entered to win ‘Dinner for Two’ at The Chef’s Table, which includes a bottle of whiskey, courtesy of Libations Mobile Catering Co. This package, valued at $150, is only available to event attendees. Each attendee will receive one raffle ticket. To increase the chance of winning, guests may buy additional raffle tickets for $20/each, which further supports the shelter animals.For more information, contact Community Events Specialist Alyssa Bean at 772-600-3215 or Events@hstc1.org.
