Zendaya Wears White Ralph Lauren Halter Dress in Italy

Publish date: 2024-01-29

Zendaya is continuing to serve up preppy tenniscore while promoting her upcoming movie Challengers. Who can forget Zendaya’s vintage 1994 Vivienne Westwood striped mini skirt set with a pink feather tail, or her white pumps with heels embellished by literal tennis balls. Following some of her and longtime stylist Law Roach’s most winning ‘fits, the Dune 2 actress debuted another sartorial ace that’s going to be living in our heads rent-free. 

This time, Zendaya channeled Cindy Crawford while stepping out for a Challengers photocall in Milan, Italy on Sunday, April 14. Posing in front of the city’s Duomo di Milano cathedral, the Euphoria star stunned in a tiny white halter dress that was previously worn by the iconic supermodel. Taken from the Ralph Lauren archives, the court-appropriate Spring 1992 collection dress was last modeled by Crawford on the runway. 

Z styled the look with minimum accessories, letting the white number’s fitted bodice, polo-style neckline, and flared miniskirt take center stage. She accentuated the country club-esque outfit with Bulgari jewelry and, a classic during her Challengers press stops, a pair of white stilettos. 

Zendaya, who’s playing a pro tennis star that becomes involved in a love triangle in her new movie, chose golden, glowing skin, smoky eyes, and a nude lip for her Milan appearance. The actress’s newly-blonde hair was styled into a voluminous retro-inspired blowout with soft waves.

With Challengers set for release on April 26, it looks like fans still have a few more weeks of preppy courtside outfits to take inspiration from. And, judging from the star’s perfect sartorial record so far, it looks like tenniscore is about to be the hottest trend for this summer.
